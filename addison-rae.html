Who is Addison Rae?

Addison Rae Easterling also known as Addison Rae is an American social media personality and actress.

She was listed by Forbes as the highest-paid TikTok personality in August 2020. The Forbes “30 Under 30” list of social media influencers included Addison Rae in 2021.

Rae began actively posting content on TikTok in 2019. Her dancing videos quickly gained popularity.

On July 31, 2022, she accumulated over 88 million followers, placing her as the fourth most-followed user on the app.

She debuted as an actress in the 2021 Netflix original movie He’s All That, a gender-flipped version of the 1999 movie She’s All That, and she also recorded her first single, “Obsessed”.

Early Life of Addison Rae

Addison Rae was born on 6th October 2001 and is 21 years of age as of 2022. She was born and raised in Lafayette, Louisiana, United States.

Rae was born to her parents Monty Easterling and Sheri Easterling. She has two younger brothers, Enzo and Lucas Lopez, and an older sister, Macy Neumeyer, who has two girls of her own.

In 2017, Addison’s parents remarried after initially divorcing when she was younger and living apart frequently throughout her childhood.

Both parents also have their TikTok accounts; Monty has 5 million followers and Sheri has 14 million followers.

Rae began dancing competitively at the age of six and has since competed all across the nation.

The dancer briefly attended Louisiana State University (LSU), where she studied sports broadcasting in the fall, but she left when she started to become well-known on TikTok.

She later moved to Los Angeles to pursue her TikTok career. Rae discusses her decision to go to Los Angeles in an interview with Elle magazine.

She stated, “A few individuals in LA reached out to me, and I was like ‘I need to go. I must leave. So I had full support from my parents”.

In the same month, Addison and her mother, “Mama Knows Best,” launched a weekly podcast that was only available on Spotify.

The podcast covered both their personal and professional lives. Rae reintroduced this podcast in 2021 under the new title “That was Fun? With Sheri and Addison.
