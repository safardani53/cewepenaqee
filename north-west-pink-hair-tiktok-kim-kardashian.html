North West, 9, shows off her new pink hair and dance moves in TikTok without famous mom Kim Kardashi

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

NORTH West, the eldest daughter of Kim Kardashian and Kanye West, has had a makeover and she looks stunning.

In a recent social media post, North and her friend took turns lip-syncing to the lyrics.

In the TikTok video, the young Kardashian (@kimandnorth) wore light pink sunglasses and a light pink shirt with an orange and yellow design. She paired it with pink makeup that looks very reminiscent of the 2000s.

On the bottom, she wore pink acid-wash denim cargo pants and massive pink sneakers.

To top it all off, North's hair also matched her outfit. She recently added several shades of pink hair to her long gorgeous braids.

Her friend gave a similar vibe when she was front and center in the camera. She wore red sunglasses with a matching red and white outfit.

The friend's pants were white with large strawberries printed all over the fabric.

The pair seemed to have a great time dancing in front of the camera and showing off their outfits in one of Kim's large closets.

TIKTOK FAMOUS

Since North is so young, Kim allowed her to have a TikTok account, but only if Kim had full access and complete control. But that hasn't stopped the spunky nine-year-old from gaining a large fan following.

The social media account has given Kardashian fans a lot to talk about, like the classic mother-daughter moment where Kim had to stop North from saying NSFW song lyrics.

The TikTok princess also found a way to push her mom into the background and make Kim a backup dancer for one of her videos.
