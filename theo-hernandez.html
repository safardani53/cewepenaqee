Who is Theo Hernandez:

Theo Hernandez is a famous Soccer Player. He was born on October 6, 1997 and his birthplace is Marseille, France. Theo is also well known as, Left back who joined Real Madrid in 2017 after beginning his professional career with Atletico Madrid. In 2018 he’d play on loan for Real Sociedad.

Theo is originated from France. He became teammates with Cristiano Ronaldo on Real Madrid.
