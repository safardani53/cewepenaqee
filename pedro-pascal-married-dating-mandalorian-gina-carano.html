Is Pedro Pascal married and who has he dated?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 15, 2024

PEDRO Pascal has become one of TV's most in-demand actors following roles in The Mandalorian, Game Of Thrones, and Wonder Woman 1984.

But fans of The Last Of Us star want to know more about his personal life - so here is what we know.

Is Pedro Pascal married and who has he dated?

As of February 2023, The Mandalorian and Game Of Thrones star, Pedro Pascal, is not married.

However, the Star Wars actor has reportedly dated a few celebrities throughout the years.

Pedro Pascal dated actress Maria Dizzia, his co-star from Law & Order, back in in 1990.

In 2014, Pedro Pascal was said to be dating his co-star from Games Of Thrones, actress Lena Headey - however, neither party confirmed the relationship.

He was then rumored to be dating Robin Tunney, his co-star from the hit TV series, The Mentalist.

Pedro was also rumored to have dated his best friend Sarah Paulson, who are often seen together on social media and at public events.

But the two have never denied or confirmed rumors that they are romantically involved.

Does Pascal have any kids?

No, Pascal does not have any children.

However, CBR reported Pascal will "likely be known as 2020s dad of the year thanks to his recent credit."

Following his role as Oberyn Martell in Game Of Thrones, Pascal has portrayed many father figures on TV.

Pascal plays Din Djarin in the hit Disney+ TV series, The Mandalorian, a bounty hunter who becomes a father figure to Grogu, a force-sensitive creature on the run.

In Wonder Woman 1984, he plays the father and con man, Maxwell Lord.

He also stars in We Can Be Heroes as Marcus Moreno, the leader of the Heroics superhero team and a dedicated single father to daughter Missy.

In real life, Pascal seems to have a great friendship with his 19-year-old co-star, Bella Ramsey, in The Last of Us. However, recently - due to an unfounded dating rumor between Leonardo DiCaprio and a 19-year-old model - a picture of Pascal and Ramsey at an event has been used in memes to compare their relationship to DiCaprio's alleged one with Eden Polani.

What is Pascal's net worth?

Pascal's net worth has been estimated to be $10million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The majority of his money comes from his many popular roles in films and on television.
