Flip or Flop's Christina Anstead and husband Ant split after ex Tarek's engagement to Selling Sunset

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 22, 2024

FLIP Or Flop’s Christina Anstead has made the “difficult decision” to split from her husband, Ant, after less than two years of marriage. 

The 37-year-old HGTV star’s split comes just weeks after her ex-husband, Tarek El Moussa, 39, got engaged to Selling Sunset’s Heather Rae Young.

Christina announced the sad news with her Instagram followers on Friday with a dark picture of her silhouette with Ant’s walking away on the beach. 

She captioned the post: “Ant and I have made the difficult decision to separate. 

“We are grateful for each other and as always, our children will remain our priority. 

“We appreciate your support and ask for privacy for us and our family as we navigate the future,” she concluded the emotional statement. 

The former couple were first linked in October 2017 and tied the knot the following year at a winter wonderland-themed wedding that took place in December at their Newport Beach home.

They exchanged vows with 70 of their closest family and friends nearby - along with their kids. 

Together, the real estate mogul and the Wheelers Dealers host welcomed son, Hudson, in September 2019.  

Christina also shares son Brayden, 5, and daughter Taylor, 9, with ex-husband and longtime Flip or Flop co-star, Tarek. 

While Ant is dad to daughter Amelie, 16, and son Archie, 14, from his previous marriage to Louise Herbert.

The California girl recently stunned in a sexy pink bikini as she celebrated her 37th birthday with Ant. 

They have frequently posted about their relationship on social media which makes the split come as a shock to fans. 

Just last month, the television presenter gushed about his “wife” in an emotional Instagram post. 

He wrote: “I say “Happy anniversary” to the Wifey EVERY SINGLE SUNDAY! Drives her crazy.”

Ant then revealed that he is going to spend everyday “spoiling his wife” as he takes a social media break. 

Just weeks before their surprise breakup, Christina’s ex, Tarek, got down on one knee and proposed to his Netflix star fiancé. 

The house flipper wrote on Instagram: "She said yes! #FlippingHerLastName.”

Heather also shared her own post as she captioned it, "The Future Mrs. Tarek El Moussa!!!!”
