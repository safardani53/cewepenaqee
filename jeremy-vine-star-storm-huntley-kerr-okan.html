Jeremy Vine star Storm Huntley stuns fans with rare loved-up snap of husband

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 14, 2024

SCOTTISH TV presenter Storm Huntley dazzled fans last week as she shared a rare loved-up snap of her and her husband.

The presenter is most known for her work with Channel 5 on their mid-morning shows but she looked stunning as she took a night off from presenting duties to glam up for a date night with her hubby.

The Jeremy Vine co-host, 36, had treated her partner to an epic birthday meal as they both flashed a grin to the camera in the snap which looked the picture of happiness.

Storm's husband, Kerr Okan, is the lead singer of Scottish rock band The LaFontaines, who have been creating music since 2008.

Kerr and his band have achieved success and have played at most major festivals in the UK and even beyond, including appearances in New York and India.
