Star Wars fans wowed by Imperial AT-AT at annual scarecrow competition in Northamptonshire village

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

STAR Wars fans were raven about this Imperial AT-AT outside a house put together for an annual scarecrow festival.

The four-legged creation, seen in film, drew crowds at the annual event in Harpole, Northamptonshire.

One entry channelled Hugh Jackman in The Greatest Showman for their scarecrow, complete with top hat and cane.

Elsewhere, an enormous Winnie the Pooh made of hay bales got the thumbs up from one young visitor.
