Millie Bobby Brown wows in green for Glamour magazines Women of the Year cover

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

ACTRESS and activist Millie Bobby Brown goes green for Glamour magazine’s Women of the Year cover.

The Stranger Things star, 19, got engaged to Jake Bongiovi, 21 — the son of rocker Jon Bon Jovi — in April, after dating for two years.

She tells the online publication: “He was a really big part of me loving myself and becoming a woman.”

The Glamour Women of the Year awards in partnership with Samsung take place in London tonight.

Millie shared a picture of herself wearing a huge ring on her engagement finger as she snuggled up to Jake in April.

Millie captioned the image: “I've loved you three summers now, honey, I want 'em all.”

While Jake shared a series of images of them from the same day, writing: “Forever.”

Their closest friends rushed to comment on it, with Pixie Lott writing: “Omg!!!! Congratulations you two so happpy for you eeeee.”

Carol Wright, who is a close family friend,  added: “Congratulations @milliebobbybrown love this.”
