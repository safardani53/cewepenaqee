Kim Zolciaks daughters Brielle &amp; Ariana praise their hot mom in new tribute after cuttin

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 22, 2024

THE daughters of Real Housewives of Atlanta star Kim Zolciak have paid tribute to her in new social media posts.

To mark Kim's 45th birthday on Friday, Ariana Biermann took to her Instagram Story and shared a snap of her and Kim in their bikinis.

Ariana captioned the post: "You are the strongest women I know!!! thank you for all you do! I am so proud of you mom! cheers to 53."

In a separate post, she said: "Happy birthday to my best friend. The hottest mom!!!!"

Meanwhile, Brielle took to her own Instagram Story on Friday and shared a similar tribute to her RHOA star mom.

"Happy birthday mom!! I love you so much!" she wrote alongside a photo of the pair holding large glasses of red wine.

Brielle, born in 1997, is Kim's eldest child from a previous relationship.

The blond star welcomed Ariana in 2001, the same year she married Daniel Toce. The couple divorced in 2003.

Kim met Kroy Biermann in 2010 and the pair married the following year in November and went on to have four children together.

In March 2013, Kroy filed to adopt Kim's daughters and in July 2013, the adoption became final and the girls subsequently changed their last names to become Brielle Biermann and Ariana Biermann.

However, a decade later and Kim has now filed for divorce from former football player Kroy.

The RHOA star reportedly called it quits, according to the legal documents obtained by TMZ.

Kim reportedly listed April 30 as the date of separation in the documents.

The Bravo star claimed the marriage was "irretrievably broken with no hope of reconciliation." 

The Don't Be Tardy star is reportedly seeking primary physical custody of the children as well as joint legal custody.

It's been reported that Brielle has cut digital ties with her adoptive father and unfollowed him on Instagram amid the divorce news.

However, he still follows Brielle and he and Ariana still follow each other on Instagram.

A few weeks ago, The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed that Kroy has also filed for divorce.

In new court docs, the former Atlanta Falcons star filed for divorce against Kim on May 8, in a shocking turn of events.

Kroy, 37, indicated that he and Kim lived together "until approximately the filing" of the complaint, which is different from what the RHOA alum wrote in her filing.

In Kroy's filing, he is also seeking "temporary and permanent sole legal and sole physical custody" of their minor children.

Together, the exes share 11-year-old Kroy Jagger, 10-year-old Kash Kade, and their twins Kaia Rose and Kane Ren, nine.

He also noted that the kids are "presently" in his "care, custody and control."
