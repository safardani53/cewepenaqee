I'm 5'11&quot; and 192 lbs - people always ask how I can be a size 12/14 but numbers mean nothing

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 23, 2024

A BODY positive influencer has shared that people don't believe her when she reveals her size.

She says that ultimately, numbers are just numbers and mean nothing.

Eden (@edenharvzofficial) shared the video with over 3 million TikTok followers.

"I'm here to show you numbers/sizes mean nothing," she said.

The content creator stood in a zip-up hoodie and shorts as she showed off her figure.

"I'm 5'11", I get questions all the time about how I'm a size 12/14," she said.

"Sometimes my trousers are even a 16 because of my fantastic butt," she joked.

"I weigh 87 kg (192 pounds), numbers and sizes mean nothing as long as you look great to you, that's all that matters," she said.

People took to the comments to share their thoughts on her message.

Women agreed with her — some shared that they varied wildly in size depending on the shop they go to.

"So true I have to get a higher size because I struggle with my thighs!" said another.

"We get put into categories too much!!"

"Absolutely love this!!! The positivity I definitely needed I won’t lie," said a third.
