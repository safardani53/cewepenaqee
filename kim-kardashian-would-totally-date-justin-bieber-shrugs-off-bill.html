Kim Kardashian Would Totally Date Justin Bieber, Shrugs Off Bill O'Reilly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 13, 2024

Appearing on last night’s edition of Lopez Tonight, Kim Kardashian has messages for two men:

  • Bill O’Reilly: Think whatever you want!
  • Justin Bieber: Call me!

    • The giant-breasted reality star was asked about these people in light of the recent Elle magazine spread that depicted her frolicking along the beach with the 16-year old singer.

    O’Reilly, the Fox News host that has never shied away from a nonsensical opinion that could yield him headlines, questioned the appropriateness of the pictorial late last week. Replied Kim to the critique:

    “Everyone is entitled to their own opinion on it. But we were just having a good time. The amazing creative team at Elle magazine obviously thought it was a great shoot and so did everyone. Everyone’s loved it. It was all in fun.”

    Not every Justin Bieber fan will love hearing this, though: Kardashian says she’d absolutely date the artist. In a couple years, at least.

     “He definitely has this swag to him,” Kim said of Justin, saying she’d only go out with Bieber if he were of “legal age.”
