What is James Gunn's net worth?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

PEACEMAKER showrunner James Gunn is one of Hollywood’s best superhero content developers.

Gunn and Peacemaker star John Cena were happy to announce their HBO Max show has been renewed for a second season after stellar reviews.

What is James Gunn's net worth?

Gunn was born in St. Louis, Missouri as the oldest of five sons and one daughter.

He and his four brothers all attended St. Louis University for undergraduate school, and James later completed a Master’s of Fine Arts at Columbia University. 

Gunn got his start by penning movies for the Scooby-Doo franchise and he's grown to be one of the most recognizable names in show business.

He is an integral asset to both Marvel and HBO Max, with superhero films and television shows dependent on his skills and attention.

Celebrity Net Worth estimates James Gunn’s net worth to be about $50million.

What movies has James Gunn directed?

Gunn’s first foray into directing superhero films came in 2010, when he wrote and directed the film Super, starring his ex-wife Jenna Fischer’s co-star Rainn Wilson in the lead role. 

The film attracted the commitment of a strong cast with Kevin Bacon, Elliot Page and Linda Cardellini but the project was far from the sweeping, otherworldly success Gunn would come to have. 

Guardians of the Galaxy spring-boarded his career in 2014, with a box office smash and the on-screen birth of characters that fans would see more of in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. 

Between the first and second Guardians films, he wrote The Belko Experiment, a harrowing and violent captivity story. 

Gunn was also the mind behind the R-rated soft-reboot of the Suicide Squad franchise. 

David Ayer’s original Suicide Squad underwhelmed fans and critics, earning just a 26% on Rotten Tomatos’ Tomatometer. 

Gunn took over the franchise, but, according to Variety, his sequel film crash landed as it was released in theaters and on HBO Max simultaneously, crippling ticket sales.

Gunn was contentiously booted from the director’s chair for Guardians of the Galaxy 3 after old, distasteful tweets were drummed up. 

Gunn was later reinstated and will direct the trilogy’s final film.

Who is Sean Gunn?

Gunn has turned some of his movies into a family affair - his brother Sean plays two roles in the Guardians franchise.

Sean’s speaking role is as Kraglin, a supporting character to Chris Pratt's Peter Quill, and he also performs the motion capture for Bradley Cooper's CGI character Rocket Raccoon.
