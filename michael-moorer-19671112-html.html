Michael Lee Moorer is a former professional American boxer who competed from 1988 to 2008. He is a multi-time world champion in two weight classes, having held the WBO light belt from 1988 to 1991; heavyweight WBO title from 1992 to 1993; titles united WBA, IBF and linear heavyweight in 1994; and the IBF heavyweight champion again from 1996 to 1997. He became the world's first left-handed heavyweight champion after defeating Evander Holyfield. Since retiring from this sports career, Moorer has worked as a boxing coach. In 2009, he worked with Freddie Roach at the Wild Card Gym in Los Angeles.
