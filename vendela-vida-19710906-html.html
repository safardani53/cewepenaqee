Vendela Vida is an American novelist, journalist, editor and educator. She is the author of several books, teacher of writing and editor-in-chief of The Believer magazine. In 2017, Vida was an Author at Lurie's Residence and a Teacher of the Faculty of English and Comparative Literature at San Jose State University. She is married to author Dave Eggers, has two children and lives in the Bay Area.

Novelist Vendela Vida was born on September 6, 1971 in California, United States (She's 52 years old now).

Vida received a Novelist Kate Chopin Award and multiple New York Times Notable Books designations for her debut novel And Now You Can Go (2003) and following works Let the Northern Lights Erase Your Name (2007), The Lovers (2010), and The Diver's Clothes Lie Empty (2015).
