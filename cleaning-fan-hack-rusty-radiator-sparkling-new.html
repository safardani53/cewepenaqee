Cleaning fan gets rusty radiator sparkling new in seconds thanks to two everyday essentials - with N

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

A CLEANING fan has revealed a quick and easy way to get chrome radiators looking sparkling new again - and there's no scrubbing involved.

The mum posted the clever hack on her Washy Wash TikTok page, with the video quickly racking up more than 287,000 views.

In the clip, she was shown using shaving gel on a wet sponge, which she applied liberally to the "dusty, rusty chrome radiator".

Once applied, she left for a minute, before removing it with a microfibre cloth.

And once the gel had been removed, the chrome radiator was sparkling clean beneath.

She was quickly praised by other cleaning fans for sharing the trick, with one person writing: "I'll be trying this tomorrow , thanks."
