Vanderpump Rules disgraced Tom Sandoval caught slamming his business partner while filming tense

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 4, 2024

TOM Sandoval has been seen filming for season 11 of Vanderpump Rules while having a serious conversation and slamming his business partner.

The Vanderpump Rules star has returned for another season of the hit Bravo spinoff following his season 10 cheating scandal.

It was revealed that Tom, 39, had been cheating on his girlfriend of nine years, Ariana Madix, with their co-star, Raquel Leviss, while shooting the previous season last year.

The fallout was captured in real-time at the beginning of March and the extra footage was tacked onto the end of season 10.

Now, Tom has been spotted talking to his best friend and business partner, Tom Schwartz, with plenty of cameras around.

A new photo and video shared by user @tanningchanning on Instagram showed the Toms conversing seriously while sitting at a high-top table in a bar.

Schwartz's back was to the camera, although he could be seen holding his head in his hand with frustration.

Across from him, Tom sported a peach-colored shirt and switched up his previous look - opting to get rid of both the mustache and white nail polish.

"Greg f**king stubs his toe and [he] ruins [everything]," Tom can be heard telling Schwartz over two bottles of beer.

The video showed two cameras in the room - one facing each of the reality stars as they seemingly talked business in regard to their Franklin Village bar, Schwartz & Sandy's.

The two opened the bar/lounge with co-owner and restauranteur Greg Morris last fall.

During season 10, both Toms were often seen disagreeing with Greg in terms of when the bar would be ready to open.

While it's been a few months, it seems as if Tom may still have some issues with Greg's business tactics.

Following the video, a photo was also shared that showed Tom clearly frustrated as he looked at the ground with his arms crossed in front of him.

"Boots on the ground for Tom v Tom fighting about their bar," the Instagram user wrote alongside the photo.

While Tom is now filming, he skipped out on the first few days of shooting in order to participate in another TV show.

The former SUR bartender was missing last Wednesday when the Bravo series started filming the new season.

Per TMZ, Tom had been out shooting the second season of Fox's Special Forces: World's Toughest Test in New Zealand.

Season 1 of the competition show aired earlier this year, with multiple celebrities being put to the test in harsh conditions to see who can complete the hard survival tasks.

While he wasn't there at the start, fans knew Tom would continue filming VPR after returning from New Zealand.

After the fallout of Scandoval, Tom spent the spring touring around the US with his band, Tom Sandoval & The Most Extras.

Recently, the band informed fans they would be taking a months-long hiatus and had to reschedule an upcoming show.

In an email sent to would-be attendees, the band said it was pushing the show back to September 30, writing that at that time "series taping will be complete."

While most of the cast has signed back on to film for season 11, one important cast member will not be returning.

WHO'S RETURNING?

All cast members have agreed to film for season 11 - except for Raquel, as reported by TMZ.

Despite the cheating scandal, Ariana has signed on to film the new season, which will undoubtfully cover the opening of the sandwich shop she owns with Katie Maloney.

As for Raquel, the former beauty queen checked into a mental health facility roughly two months ago following the filming of the season 10 reunion at the end of March.

According to production, Raquel's team has been working hard to get her back on the show for the new season.

However, she is currently still at the facility and has been making her mental health a priority as she continues her social media hiatus.

END OF SEASON 10

At the very end of the season 10 reunion, Raquel made a shocking confession about the timeline of her affair with Tom.

It was revealed that six days after the reunion was filmed in March, as the cast sat down to do their final confessionals, Raquel decided to come clean about the timeline of her affair.

Raquel confirmed several of the lies that Tom said about their affair after being interrogated by her co-stars, including their "one-night stand" in early August, six days after the reunion was taped.

She admitted that she and Tom had hooked up multiple times during Scheana Shay's wedding in August.

Raquel also admitted to hooking up with Tom in his shared house with Ariana while she was away for her grandmother's funeral, despite his furious denial during the reunion taping prior to her one-on-one interview.

“I love Ariana as a person and then also I’m in love with Tom Sandoval, so yeah it didn’t seem that far-fetched of an idea, but it was not something in question. God, he’s gonna kill me I just hate lying so much,” Raquel said.
