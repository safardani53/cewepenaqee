Andrés Iniesta Luján is a Spanish professional footballer who currently plays for Vissel Kobe in the J1 League. Iniesta, regarded as one of the best midfielders of all time, spent most of his career with Barcelona, where he was captain for three seasons.

What is Andres Iniesta’s current salary & Net Worth?

Andres Iniesta has a staggering net worth of $120 million throughout his football career. Similarly, the Spaniard was involved in a number of partnerships and endorsements. According to Forbes, he will earn roughly $3 million in 2020 through sponsorship agreements and marketing.

In 2018, Iniesta signed a multi-year partnership with Asics. He was named brand ambassador by the organization. As a result, insiders estimated the sale to be in the millions. Aside from sponsorships, his career earnings are tremendous. Between the 2020 and 2021 seasons, Iniesta earns $26.6 million. Iniesta spent 22 years at Barcelona, his sole club in Europe. His weekly wage was as low as $8,000 at first.

In 2018, he moved to Vissel Kobe in Japan. According to Bleacher Report, Iniesta was paid around $600,000 every week. He was also under a three-year deal with the club. As a result, he expected to earn $30 million from Kobe alone in a year.

Vissel Kobe’s Deal with Andres Iniesta

Iniesta signed a three-year contract with Japanese club Vissel Kobe on May 24, 2018. On 22 July, he made his debut as a second-half replacement for Kazuma Watanabe in a 0-3 loss to Shonan Bellmare. Iniesta signed a contract extension with Vissel Kobe on May 11, 2021, keeping him at the club for another two seasons.
