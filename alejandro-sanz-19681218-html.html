Alejandro Sanz (World Music Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

Alejandro Sánchez Pizarro, better known as Alejandro Sanz, is a Spanish musician, singer and songwriter. Sanz has won 22 Latin Grammy Awards and 4 Grammy Awards. He has won the Latin Grammy Award for Album of the Year three times, more than any other artist. The singer is known for his flamenco-influenced ballads and has also experimented with several other genres including pop, rock, funk, R&B and jazz. Born in Madrid to Andalusian parents, Sanz started playing guitar at the age of seven, influenced by his family's flamenco roots. Sanz released his first album at the age of 19, although he did not achieve commercial success in Spain until his second release, Viviendo Deprisa. His next two records, Si Tu Me Miras and 3 also achieved high commercial sales, but it was his 1997 album, Más, that achieved international success. This was followed by El Alma al Aire in 2000, which sold over a million copies in its first week.
