Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Sander van Dijck, known by his stage name San Holo, is a Dutch DJ, musician, record producer and composer from Zoetermeer. He has gained international recognition for his remix "The Next Episode" by Dr. Dre, currently has more than 232 million views on YouTube. He has released music on several record companies, including Spinnin 'Records, OWSLA, Barong Family and Monstercat. He also founded Bitbird, a label that released several of his singles including "Still Looking" and "Light" and his debut album Album1. San Holo's debut EP, Cosmos, was released through Heroic on September 18, 2014 and is on iTunes' Top 100 Electronic category. San Holo's debut album, Album1, was released via Bitbird on September 21, 2018 and ranked 7th on the Billboard Dance / Electronic Albums chart. In 2017, he was nominated for the Electronic Music Awards for New Artist of the Year.
