GETTING your barbecue started can be time-consuming and difficult - so being equipped with the best BBQ lighter on hand will make things easier and safer for you.

How to light a BBQ without lighter fluid

Using lighter fluid might seem like the easiest way to get a barbecue started but it can be dangerous and, because it doesn’t burn away completely, it can end up in your food.

While the level of toxicity is low, it’s definitely not good for you.

It’s not hard to start a barbecue without lighter fluid once you get the hang of it though.

  • Build a loose mound of coals in the base of your barbecue remembering to let air circulate.
  • In the gaps, stuff scrunched newspaper or natural firefighters (wood, wool, or similar from Homefire).
  • Light the newspaper or the firelighters and wait for the coals to catch - a sheltered position away from the wind is ideal.
  • The coals are ready when they’ve turned grey.
  • Now you're ready to distribute the lit coals.

    • How to use a BBQ chimney lighter

    If you barbecue regularly, it’s worth investing in a BBQ chimney lighter like this one from Weber, which makes it easy to light coals even on a windy day or if you don’t have a sheltered spot.

    Here is how it's done:

  • Place the coals on the top.
  • Put the firelighters beneath the chimney (scrunched-up newspapers are best).
  • The flames will travel upwards and light the coals.
  • Once the coals are grey they're ready.
  • Tip them into your barbecue (make sure you have good pair of heat-proof gloves for this bit).
  • Voila! You're ready to barbeque.

    How to refill a BBQ lighter

    If you have a butane gas lighter there will come a time when you need to refill it, as with any gas lighter - but it is a lot easier than you may think.

    Here is a quick step-by-step guide:

  • First, either remove the bottom of the lighter or locate the pinhole at the base of your model.
  • Then insert the pin of the lighter gas refill canister into the hole at the bottom of the lighter.
  • Hold it for just 3 seconds to fill the lighter and remove it.
  • It's always best to wait 2 minutes after refilling before you start using your lighter.
  • Hey presto! You're all set.
