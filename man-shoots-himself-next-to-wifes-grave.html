Pensioner, 83, dies after 'shooting himself in the head at wife's grave' in New York cemetery

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

A WIDOWER has died after shooting himself in the head next to his wife's grave at a cemetery.

Robert Gallo, 83, shot himself at St John's Cemetery, Queens, New York just after 3pm local time yesterday.

A cemetery worker heard the gunshot and found Gallo lying next to his wife’s black marble headstone, according to Brooklyn/Queens Diocese spokesman.

He said: “Many of the employees at the cemetery know the man because he visits his late wife’s grave often.

“We are saddened about what happened and are praying for him and his family.”

Medics rushed him to Elmhurst Hospital Centre, where he died after a short stay in the intensive care unit, reports the New York Post.

A .22-calibre pistol was resting by his side at the cemetery in Metropolitan Ave, Middle Village, Queens.
