‘One Tree Hill’ Reunion

Our dreams came true when the cast of One Tree Hill headed back to Wilmington, North Carolina on March 28. While Chad Michael Murray, Hilarie Burton and tons of others showed up, one Tree Hill resident was noticeably missing — Sophia!
