Multi-millionaire Paris Fury proudly shows off her new winter PJsand theyre from Primark

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 10, 2024

SHE may be worth millions, but that doesn't stop Paris Fury from shopping at one of our favourite bargain high-street stores.

The mum-of-six, 32, who is married to heavyweight boxing champion Tyson Fury, took to Instagram and shared a snap of herself posing in the mirror wearing a set of adorable Winnie the Pooh-themed pyjamas from Primark.

Alongside the selfie, she penned: "OK, I got 15 minutes in the bath with Valencia running in twice asking me to put her back to bed.

"But I did have my new Winnie the Pooh winter pjs to get into."

The full-length soft touch pyjamas, which feature a fun print of Winnie the Pooh's head, are perfect to snuggle into for the winter months and are from Primark's most recent Disney range.

And it's not the first time Paris had revealed that she enjoys a bargain just as much as the rest of us...

Despite living in a £550k Morecambe house which features six bathrooms, golden thrones & £50k walk-in shoe closet, the glamorous mum sent shoppers into a frenzy after posing in a skimpy top and jeans on her Instagram page back in June.
