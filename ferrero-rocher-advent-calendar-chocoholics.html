Ferrero Rocher advent calendar now costs 8 - and it's perfect for chocoholics

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 8, 2024

THIS will make you go nutty for Ferrero Rochers.

This year, chocoholics can get a Ferrero advent calendar dedicated to the tasty gold-wrapped treats – and it looks like a game-changer.

  • Ferrero Golden Gallery advent calendar, £8 from Debenhams - buy now

Inside the 25 doors of the Ferrero Golden Gallery advent calendar is an assortment of different chocolate treats by the brand.

Of course the calendar had to include some offerings of their iconic Ferrero Rochers, but there are other chocolates concealed behind the doors too.

Fans can also tuck into three Cappuccinos, four Tenderly Biancoretti, four Tenderly Torroncino, four Delicius, three Manderly and three Tenderly Dark.

And thankfully, the confectionery calendar doesn’t break the bank and costs £8 from Debenhams.
