Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

Timothee Chalamet and Kylie Jenner are one of the hottest, but unexpected celebrity couples to emerge in 2023. Although they’ve been pictured together before, they’ve been keeping things on the down low. Fans aren’t sure just how serious they are, but some eagle-eyed viewers think they spotted the Wonka actor lurking in the background of their famous Christmas Eve bash.

The couple first sparked rumors they were seeing each other in April, as some joked momager Kris Jenner was behind the ‘PR move’. However, the pair then went public at Beyoncé’s star-studded birthday concert and again engaged in some PDA at the US Open. Now, fans think they’ve seen the actor with Kylie Jenner at the family Christmas party, so things may be getting more serious than once thought.

Timothee Chalamet ‘spotted’ at The Kardashians Christmas Eve party

From Kourtney’s gorgeous post-baby look to the family TikTok (minus Kim), the event was documented for all to see on social media.

However, one thing that wasn’t documented was the ‘arrival’ of Kylie’s date, Timothee. But it looks like Kim Kardashian taught fans well as they’ve picked up her detective skills and seemingly spotted him chatting to the 26-year-old in the background.

Taking to X, formerly known as Twitter, eagle-eyed fans spotted the actor in the background of a Snapchat posted by Landon Barker, Kourtney’s 20-year-old stepson.

Although not very clear, fans spotted what looked like Timothee’s signature brown locks, as he leaned over engaging in conversation with the youngest KarJenner sister.

“LANDON’S SNAPCHAT!!! Once again the Barker kids are giving us the tea,” wrote one excited fan.

“Can they stop hiding and give us clearer pics ’cause I need it!!” exclaimed another.

“I knew it. He HAD to be there,” a third wrote.
