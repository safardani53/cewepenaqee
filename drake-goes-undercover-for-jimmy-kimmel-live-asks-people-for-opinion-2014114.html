Drake Goes Undercover for Jimmy Kimmel Live, Asks People for Opinion

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

And nothing was the same for Fake Drake. Rapper Drake (Aubrey Drake Graham) went incognito Thursday, April 10, for a takeover of “Lie Witness News” on Jimmy Kimmel Live!

The “Started From the Bottom” rapper, 27, wearing a fake beard, wig, and hipster-style frames, interviewed random people on the streets of Hollywood, asking them what they thought of him.

One supportive fan said Drake “definitely killed” the ESPYs, which the Degrassi alum was announced as the 2014 host on Wednesday, April 9. Drake then asked another person how he felt about the rapper’s refusal to acknowledge Caucasian athletes at the ESPYs: “I think it’s baloney… hopefully it will impact his career, too, in a negative way,” the man replied.

The only problem? The ESPYS 2014 have yet to take place and will air on July 16.

The incognito hip hop star then asked another man in a floral-printed top for his opinion of Drake , “I don’t like Drake, I think he’s an actor, he’s not a real rapper.” The musician then improvised a song and screeched, “Drake, hey, I think you suck!”

During the final part of Fake Drake’s “Lie Witness News” segment, he asked one of his defenders how he would react if the rapper was with him at that very moment. His young fan responded, “I would freak out,” while Drake slowly peeled off his beard, took off his glasses and eventually, his wig.
