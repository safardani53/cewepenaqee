Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 20, 2024

JANELLE Brown has left her husband, Kody, according to a shocking video.

In the clip, Christine Brown's daughter Gwendlyn, 21, claimed that Janelle has already left the Sister Wives patriarch.

While answering fans questions on TikTok, Gwendolyn's friend Sarah dropped the bombshell.

"Everyone thinks Janelle is going to leave your dad," Sarah said while lying down. "Well she already did leave, I'll speak out on that."

In the background, Gwendolyn could be heard confirming the news.

"Yes! Go mom!" she cried out. "She got out of that. It was not a good relationship for her."

PREVIOUS CLUES

Last month, fans thought Janelle sent a secret message hinting that she was about to dump Kody.

Janelle, 53, shared the cryptic message in an Instagram post directed to her fans.

The post features a closeup selfie photo of the TLC star as she held a paper coffee cup.

The snap was captioned, "Have you ever had a situation that you were stressed would happen when traveling?"

Janelle continued: "After Maddie and I got on the road to come back to NC I realized I had left my driver's license in the clutch I took to the wedding. I’m usually so neurotic about things like that I can’t believe this happened!"

The star concluded the explainer, writing: "Sometimes you are afraid of something but when it happens you find out you really are capable enough to adapt and deal. Maybe there is a life lesson in that!"

Fans of the series reposted the photo along with its caption on a Sister Wives dedicated Reddit board.

The post was titled" "Could this be a hidden meaning in Janelle's IG post this morning?"

The fan's comment "at the end" read: "To the life lesson you mentioned... fear is worse than the actual event sometimes."

To which Janelle responded with a "clinking glasses" emoji.

CHRISTINE WAS FIRST

Janelle may have joined Christine, who already left Kody.

In an August issue of PEOPLE, Christine revealed the final straw that ended their troubled marriage.

"He broke my little girl's heart," she told the magazine.

In 2020, Christine and Kody's daughter Ysabel, now 19, had to have major surgery to correct her scoliosis.

However, the specialist for the procedure was located in New Jersey, forcing them to travel far from their Utah home during the height of the pandemic.

Kody, concerned about traveling during the Covid pandemic, refused to go with them.

"I understood Covid, and why he couldn't come, but she didn't," Christine reflected. "And she still has a really hard time with it.
