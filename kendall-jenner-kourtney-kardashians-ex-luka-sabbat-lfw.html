Kendall Jenner cozies up to sister Kourtney Kardashian's ex Luka Sabbat at London Fashion Week party

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 28, 2024

KENDALL Jenner cozied up to actor Luka Sabbat as she partied during London Fashion Week following her Burberry runway appearance yesterday.

The supermodel shared several pictures on Instagram from her evening, including one of her getting close with the Grown-Ish star, 22, who previously dated her sister Kourtney, 40, in 2018.

Another shot saw the 24-year-old sitting on a couch by herself, clad in a long-sleeved butterfly print top with daring criss-cross detailing in the middle and a matching maxi skirt.

While another picture saw the E! star posing up a storm with fellow model Mona Tougaard.

Captioning the photos, she wrote: "After Burberry".

Also posting to her Instagram stories, the model shared another picture of Sabbat in a black suit, white dress shirt and tie.

Previously it was reported that Kendall's sister Kourtney and Luka had dated in September 2018, after they were spotted going for dinner at L.A. hot spot The Nice Guy.

Although they were seen together on several occasions over the next few weeks, a source told People that there was "nothing serious" between them.

A source told the publication at the time: "It makes her feel good to have these hot, young guys who are interested in her.

"Luka is also good friends with Kendall... He’s young and is going with the flow.

"If everyone’s saying, 'Hang out with Kourtney,' he’s going to hang out with her."

Catwalk star Kendall was recently seen soaking up the sun with NBA star beau Ben Simmons in Miami following the Super Bowl.

Elsewhere, Kendall commanded attention earlier in the evening as she walked in the Burberry Fall/Winter runway show while a plethora of stars, including Luka, watched on from the FROW.

Following her dazzling display on the runway, Kendall attended the Renell Medrano and WePresent opening preview of PAMPARA Photographic exhibition.

She was later pictured arriving to the LOVE magazine party at The Standard Hotel alongside Bella Hadid and Kaia Gerber.
