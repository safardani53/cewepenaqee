Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 24, 2024

AMERICAN Pickers star Mike Wolfe's girlfriend, Leticia Cline, has shared some new, never-before-seen pics with him.

The 44-year-old former Playboy model took to her Instagram account to post a few throwback images.

Among them were a group photo that Leticia posed in alongside Mike and rock star Jack White.

The image was taken from August, when the American Pickers cast filmed an episode with Jack.

She captioned the image: "Since I'm in Detroit let's take a stroll down memory lane from last year when we were here…"

She also posed on top of a wooden sign that read "Wolff-American High Art Cycles" with Mike on her other side.
