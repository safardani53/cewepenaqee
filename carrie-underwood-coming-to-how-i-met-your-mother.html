Carrie Underwood Coming to How I Met Your Mother

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 10, 2024

She just got engaged in real life. Now Carrie Underwood is set to appear on How I Met Your Mother, a comedy in which a single guy searches for his wife!

Might Underwood be “The Mother” that Josh Radnor’s Ted Mosby tells his kids about 20 years in the future (while sounding eerily like Bob Saget)? Doubtful.

As far we know, Carrie is only scheduled for one How I Met Your Mother episode as Tiffany, an enigmatic pharmaceutical sales rep and Ted love interest.

That’s probably just as well. She’s got a country music career to focus on, and besides, her actual fiance, NHL star Mike Fisher, might take issue with Ted!

Carrie Underwood will appear on the show in March.

Even with her short stint, Carrie joins a long line of recent HIMYM guest stars including Joanna Garcia, Heidi Montag, Kim Kardashian and Britney Spears.

The CBS comedy returns Monday for its 100th episode, when Stacy Keibler and Rachel Bilson, who some fans think has Mother potential, will also appear.
