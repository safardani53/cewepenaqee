Post Malone expecting first child with longtime girlfriend after years-long 'secret romance'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

POST Malone is expecting his first child.

The singer revealed to TMZ that his longtime girlfriend is pregnant with their first baby.

Speaking to the outlet, 26-year-old Post said: "I'm excited for this next chapter in my life, I'm the happiest I've ever been, and for since I could remember I was sad.

"Time to take care of my body and my family and friends, and spread as much love as we can every day."

Sources close to the singer say that he and his girlfriend celebrated the big news over the weekend with family and friends in Southern California.

It's an exciting time for the Rockstar hitmaker, whose fourth album Twelve Carat Toothache will be released June 3.

The news may come as a surprise to some fans, given that Post has kept his relationship very private over the years.

According to TMZ, his girlfriend has not spent any time in the spotlight.

He was previously romantically linked to concert promoter Ashen Diaz, and Korean singer and visual artist MLMA, short for Me Love Me A lot.

WHO IS POST MALONE?

Born Austin Richard Post, he is an American singer, songwriter, actor and rapper.

He was born in Syracuse, New York, and moved to Grapevine, Texas, at the age of nine.
