Teen, 18, stunned after buying a McDonald's doughnut - but warned to wait 'a few hours' to eat it as

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 12, 2024

A STUNNED teenager claims she received a note with her McDonald's order warning her to wait a few hours before eating her doughnut - because it was still FROZEN.

Danni Jordan was left bemused when she found the note attached to her millionaire's doughnut that she ordered from the St. Peter's Street branch in Derby on Monday.

The note read: "Hi, I'm very sorry that this doughnut is still frozen... please wait a few hours before eating."

The 18-year-old only spotted the note a few hours after receiving her delivery and had not got round to eating the sweet treat after finishing her meal.

Danni, from Derby, said: "I couldn't stop laughing. It was so random and I couldn't believe they'd sent me a frozen doughnut.

"I didn't see the note for about an hour and by that point, it was pretty defrosted but the caramel was still pretty frozen."

She added: "It didn't really taste that much different. It was slightly soggy when defrosted, but it still tasted nice.

"The doughnuts are so nice that I can't not order them - I'd prefer it if they were defrosted though."

Danni, who lives with her partner Josh, doesn't think she'll change her views on the fast food chain after the mishap.

She said: "I tweeted a picture of the note to McDonald's but didn't get a response.

"It's more funny than complaint worthy so I didn't think to ring the actual restaurant or anything."

McDonald's have been approached for comment.
