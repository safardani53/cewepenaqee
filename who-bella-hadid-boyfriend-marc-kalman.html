Who is Bella Hadid's boyfriend, Marc Kalman?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

SUPERMODEL Bella Hadid went public with Marc Kalman in July 2021 following her high-profile split from The Weeknd.

Now, fans want to know more about her man and his career.

Who is Bella Hadid's boyfriend, Marc Kalman?

Like Bella, Marc is also known for his career in the fashion industry, however, he often stays behind the scenes.

As an art director, he has dabbled in merchandise design, album art, and branding and he has also worked with several high-profile clients, including Travis Scott, KNG Records, and the eyewear brand Smoke x Mirrors, according to Seventeen.

Additionally, Kalman served as a fashion assistant for The New York Times Style Magazine.

Outside of his occupation, not much is known about his personal life because he often stays out of the spotlight, however, he does make appearances on Bella's Instagram page.

How did Bella Hadid and Marc Kalman meet?

Bella and Marc's fairy tale romance began in New York City.

In July 2021, a source told E! News that they "had met before in New York City while working."

"He has been the creative behind many fashion projects and they have crossed paths several times," the source added.

The pair reportedly started dating in 2020 but did not go public until 2021.

"She's introduced him to her family and they all think he's a great guy," the source continued.

Who has Bella Hadid dated?

Prior to Bella's relationship with Marc, she was linked to several high-profile celebrities, however, her only confirmed relationship was with The Weeknd, which started in May 2015 and ended in August 2019.

Bella has also been linked to rapper Drake and football star Odell Beckham Jr.

She also dated a guy by the name of Matthew Morton before she became famous, according to PopSugar.
