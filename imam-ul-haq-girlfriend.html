Details on Imam-Ul-Haq Girlfriend, Wife and Dating History

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 20, 2024

Imam-ul-Haq, born 22 December 1995, is a Pakistani cricketer. The Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) awarded him a central contract for the 2018-19 season in August 2018.

In 2019, he was all over the news after a woman accused him of misdemeanors, shared WhatsApp chats, and later got involved in a big scandal.

Since then, people have expressed an interest in learning more about his personal life.

Imam-Ul-Haq dating history and controversy

Imam is currently single. However, according to several sources, the left-handed batsman has several affairs.

However, there is no information available about the ladies he dated because he guarded his privacy.

Later, he landed in a scandal when his personal conversations were leaked on social media.

A Twitter user shared some alleged chats of Imam with many girls, accusing the Pakistan batsman of using his fame to take advantage of them.

According to the Twitter user, Imam is dating 7–8 girls, and the girls directly exchanged screen images of Imam’s talks with her.

As shown in the chats, Imam is getting personal with a girl and addressing her by nickname.

Imam-Ul-Haq personal chat screenshot (Source: Mykhel)

In another chat, he was shown having a furious argument with a girl about a breakup.

Imam-Ul-Haq personal chat got leaked (Source: Mykhel)

The girls alleged that Imam keeps them in the dark about his romantic affairs.

A lot of supporters showed a neutral response, while others were disappointed.

Fans response on Imam-Ul-Haq Scandal (Source: Twitter)

Many Twitter users have also come out to say that the controversy is not related to the #MeToo controversy because Imam did not harass or threaten anyone, and the connections were mutual.

Others stated, “Regardless of how you classify Imam’s behavior, lying for sex and intimacy in a society like ours is disgraceful and dangerous.”

After this incident attracted much attention, the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) first refused to comment, claiming it was a player’s personal matter.

Later, PCB Managing Director Wasim Khan stated that Imam was “remorseful and has apologized” for his actions.

“Imam was remorseful and apologized for everything that had happened, as well as admitting his mistake.” He said that the situation became out of hand owing to a misunderstanding.

“However, we made it clear to him that, while this is his personal and private matter, we expect our players to uphold the highest standards of ethics and discipline.”

He also added stating, “Hopefully, we will not see such incidents again.”

Imam-Ul-Haq reveals the qualities he is looking for in a life companion

Imam recently appeared on one of the episodes of Hassan Choudhary’s The Talk Talk Show, where Hassan questioned the question, “What qualities do you seek in your future wife?”

Imam said, “There should be the element of respect between the two. We should both feel equal in the partnership. Aside from that, she should be nice and outgoing like myself.”

Imam made an appearance on an episode of Hasna Mana Hai a year ago, where he received a marriage proposal from a lady.

He blushed and turned to the girl, claiming that he could not answer the question and that it should be directed to his mother, who could respond on his behalf.

Imam-Ul-Haq’s cricket career

He has represented Pakistan’s Under-19 and Under-23 teams, as well as Balochistan, Habib Bank Limited, Lahore Region Blues, and Peshawar Zalmi in the Pakistan Super League.

Imam-Ul-Haq dedicated his Player of the Series trophy to his parents (Source: Instagram)

In the final match of the 2016-17 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy, he scored 200 runs not out for Habib Bank Limited and 59 runs not out in the final match of the 2017 National T20 Cup, which his club Lahore Blues won.

On May 11, 2018, Imam made his test debut against Ireland at the Malahide Cricket Club Ground in Dublin, Ireland. In his debut encounter, he hit 74 runs not out in the second innings, pushing Pakistan to victory.

He scored 157 runs in the first innings and 111 runs not out in the second innings of a match against Australia in 2022.

During the second innings of the match, he made a partnership of 252 runs for the first wicket with Abdullah Shafique, which became Pakistan’s highest partnership against Australia for any wicket.

His incredible effort in the match won him the title of Man of the Match.

Imam-Ul-Haq century list

Imam, the talented Pakistani batsman, has notched up an impressive list of centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs).

One of his standout centuries came on March 31, 2022, when he scored a brilliant 106 runs against Australia in Lahore, followed closely by another century just two days prior, with a superb 103 runs.

These Back-to-back centuries showed his consistency and ability to perform against strong opponents.

Imam-Ul-Haq playing a match against West Indies (Source: Instagram)

However, Imam’s prowess with the bat is not limited to recent years. In July 2019, during a match against Bangladesh in London, he scored a flawless century, registering 100 runs while facing 100 balls

One of his most memorable ODI innings was his remarkable 151 runs against England in Bristol in May 2019.

Imam’s list of ODI centuries also includes noteworthy performances against South Africa, Zimbabwe, and Sri Lanka.

His century against South Africa in Centurion in January 2019, where he scored 101 runs, showcased his adaptability to different conditions and opponents.

Additionally, his back-to-back centuries against Zimbabwe in Bulawayo in July 2018, with scores of 110, 113, and 128 runs, illustrated his ability to dominate the opposition consistently.

Imam-Ul-Haq age, height, and weight

At such a young age, the Imam has achieved enormous achievements. He will be 28 years old in 2023. 

He stands 5 feet 7 inches tall and weighs 71 kg (156 lbs). Furthermore, he has dark black hair and black eyes.
