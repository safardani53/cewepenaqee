Rebel Wilson goes shirtless in sexy sports bra before beach run as she nears her weight loss goal af

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 29, 2024

REBEL Wilson went shirtless in a sexy sports bra before hitting the beach to go on a run.

The outing comes as she nears her weight loss goal after losing 40 lbs.

The 40-year-old actress showed off her slim figure in an Instagram post on Friday.

Rebel donned a serious expression while rocking a sports bra, black biker shorts, a pink baseball cap and sunglasses.

The Pitch Perfect star captioned the post: "What up BE-AtCH! (Woke up early, ready to smash a beach run)."

Back in January, Rebel announced that 2020 would be her “Year of Health" as she changed her lifestyle entirely with surfing and refusing to use the word "diet."

She shared that turning 40 inspired her to live a more healthy lifestyle.

The actress exclusively told The Sun: "Weirdly this year was always going to be the year of health. I've been naming my years now, and, that's kind of having these resolutions but for the whole year."

Rebel said: “It’s not like I want to lose weight and get to a certain number. It’s more than that.

“It’s about dealing mentally with why I was overeating and I had a job where I was paid a lot of money to be bigger at times, which can kind of mess with your head a bit.”

The Australian actress has been proudly flaunting her weight loss on Instagram.

In late October, Rebel joked that she was "stretching it out before work" as she wore black Burberry leggings ahead of an interview with Tiny Talk Show.

She paired the stirrup leggings with a black and white print shirt in the Instagram post.

She recently revealed to her fans that she is six pounds away from her goal of 165 pounds.

In addition to her impressive weight loss, she has found love with 29-year-old Jacob Busch.

According to reports, the two started dating after they were introduced by a friend last year.
