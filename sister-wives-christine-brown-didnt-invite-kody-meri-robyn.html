Sister Wives' Christine Brown snubs Kody, Robyn and Meri from birthday bash as fans praise her 'sava

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

CHRISTINE Brown has snubbed her ex-husband, Kody Brown, and sister wives, Meri and Robyn, from her birthday bash.

The Sister Wives star left the polygamist relationship in November 2021.

In an exclusive clip obtained by Access Hollywood, Christine, 51, and her daughters, Mykelti, 27, and Aspyn, 28, sat around the kitchen table to talk about her birthday party.

The three girls decided the bash should be 1950s-themed since Christine was turning 50 at the time.

Things got a bit awkward when they discussed who would be invited to the party.

In a confessional, Christine revealed that she wouldn't invite Kody, 54, Meri, 52, or Robyn, 44, and shrugged her shoulders.

"So many of our family gatherings were tense," the TV personality said reminiscing on old memories, "I didn't feel like I was really a wife, I just felt like a second-class citizen a lot of the time.

"So I really am only going to invite people that I really want to hang out with and that it's going to be easy and relaxing and no drama."

However, she was excited to invite her fellow sister wife Janelle, 54, as the two are still very close.

Christine also invited the other kids that she still had contact with.

SAYING GOODBYE

Christine was the first wife to leave the relationship after 25 years of marriage.

Janelle and Meri followed suit and announced their breakups during a Sister Wives special that aired in December 2022.

Kody is now down to one wife: Robyn

Christine has six children with Kody: Aspyn, Mykelti, Paedon, 24, Gwendlyn, 21, Ysabel, 20, and Truely, 13.

NEW BEGINNINGS

Since her separation from Kody, Christine has since moved on.

In April, she revealed that she was engaged to her fiance, David Woolley.

On Thursday during an Instagram Live with Janelle, she revealed that her wedding was in about a month.

She also discussed losing eight more pounds before the big day.

She showed off her slender frame as she sported a green sweater, gold earrings, and a pair of cat eyeglasses while addressing her followers.

She dished that she loses weight "most successfully" through the support she receives and will soon start a Facebook group that will track the progress of its members while also motivating each other to follow through with their weight loss goals..

Christine announced that it's "all through Plexus" and that the program will be called "Shred the Rest."

The TV personality said that the only way fans can join the group is by signing up and receiving their first order of Plexus, which is a weight loss supplement company.
