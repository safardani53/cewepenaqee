Who Is Andrew Walker Married To? Meet Hallmark Stars Wife

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Andrew Walker is a talented American actor best known for his performances in numerous acting roles such as Radio Active, Maybe It’s Me, ER, The Big Bang Theory, The Gundown, Ambush at Dark Canyon, and Psychophonia.

In addition, he has made a name by appearing in over 20 Hallmark movies, including A Bride for Christmas, A Dream of Christmas, Appetite for Love, Christmas Tree Lane, An Unexpected Christmas, and Dying for Chocolate: A Curious Caterer Mystery.

Similarly, he has appeared in one Hallmark TV show, When Calls the Heart. In most of those Hallmark Channel television movies, Walker tends to portray the character of a man looking for love.

But, if we speak of the actor in real life, he is actually in a blissful marital relationship with his wife, Cassandra Troy Walker, aka Cassandra Troy — an entrepreneur, adventurer, and fitness enthusiast. Here is a look at his married life!

How long has Andrew Walker been married?

Walker first met his future wife, Troy, in early 2004. As Troy explained in her Instagram post of January 10, 2021, it all started after they discovered their mutual love for nature and went on a hiking trip to Mont-Tremblant.

“In the first week we met (circa 2004!), we discovered each others love for nature. It started with a hiking trip in @monttremblant followed by countless small wilderness adventures, and some big big trips which are always centered around being in nature,” she had written.

So the now-married couple met around 2004; their love for nature brought them closer, and after getting to know each other closely, they eventually started dating.

As years passed by, Walker and Troy’s relationship became stronger and stronger. There seems to be only one thing lacking — a wedding. That was also fulfilled when they walked down the aisle and shared the wedding vows on June 7, 2012.

Since their wedding, the couple has always been together and helped and supported each other no matter what. Soon after their marriage, in 2013, they even launched a locally-sourced and cold-pressed juice brand, Clover Juice, based in Los Angeles, West Hollywood.

Andrew Walker with his wife, Cassandra Troy, in 2023 (Photo: Instagram)

On June 7, 2014, Troy marked her second wedding anniversary with her husband via Instagram. She shared a selfie taken at a beach and wrote, “Happy 2 years to my favorite partner in crime.”

Several more happy instances could be found on the couple’s respective Instagram handles. And all of them depict the two’s blissful married life, filled with love, respect, and several adventures they have gone on together.

As of now, Walker has been together with his wife for around 19 years, out of which they have spent 11 years as a married couple. Troy marked her 11th wedding anniversary with her husband with a special post on Instagram.

Two days after their official 11th wedding anniversary and on Walker’s 44th birthday, on June 9, 2023, Troy posted a picture with her life partner and wrote a heartfelt caption. She mentioned that she is grateful every day that they get to spend this lifetime together.

“… and hopefully many many more lifetimes. Oh, and happy 11-year anniversary to us… and 19 years together. Time [flies] when you’re having fun!!” she added. Furthermore, she concluded her caption by adding, “ILY.”

Andrew Walker and Cassandra Troy have two children

Andrew and Troy have been blessed with two kids in their 11-year-long marital relationship. Both of their children are sons; they are named West Byron Walker and Wolf Reinhard Walker. The couple’s first child is West Byron Walker, born on July 16, 2015.

More than three years after welcoming their first child, the duo decided to relaunch their juice brand under a new name. Their juice brand was initially named Clover Juice, which they opened from the savings they made by not having a grand wedding.

They named their juice brand Little West, and as per Troy, she and Andrew chose the name after their eldest son, West, and their shared love of the west coast. “As excited as we are about the new name and look, our process hasn’t changed at all,” she added.

Andrew Walker and his wife, Cassandra Troy Walker, with their sons West Byron Walker and Wolf Reinhard Walker (Photo: Instagram)

On July 12, 2019, Troy announced on Instagram that she was pregnant with her second child with her spouse. On December 26, 2019, she revealed that they had welcomed a baby boy named Wolf Reinhard Walker.

After the birth of their second son, the couple dived back into parenting mode and have been raising their kids in the best way they can. It’s reported that the couple, who are from Montreal, and their kids currently reside in Los Angeles.

As of now, Andrew and Troy are still together, and their relationship is still strong as it used to be. Furthermore, their children have also grown and could be seen being featured on their parents’ social media handles, where both West and Wolf look happy and enjoying.

Andrew Walker’s short bio and net worth

Born in 1979, Andrew celebrates his birthday every year on June 9, making him 44 years of age as of 2023. His birthplace is Montreal, Quebec, and his birth name is Andrew William Walker.

Speaking of his parents, his father’s name is Bruce Walker, and his mother’s name is Joyce Walker. While his father works as a school administrator, his mother — who has passed away — worked as a librarian. He also has a sister named Jenimay Walker, an actress and model.

Coming over to his earnings, Celebrity Net Worth reports that he has an estimated net worth of $2 million. The Vanier College graduate has had a successful acting career, with 83 acting projects credited in his IMDb profile, including his two upcoming gigs.
