Ramona Singer has net worth of millions despite Real Housewives stars divorce Sun 24 Decembe

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 9, 2024

The Real Housewives of New York City was revived this year and revamped with a whole new cast. But, fans won’t ever forget the show’s original housewives including Luann de Lesseps and Ramona Singer. As Ramona returns to screens for The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip in 2023, her net worth is thrown into question.

During her 13 seasons on The Real Housewives of New York City, Ramona had some unforgettable moments. When the now-67-year-old first appeared on the Bravo show she was married to Mario Singer. However, her life looks a little different now as she heads to stunning St Barts for The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip with Dorina Medley, Kristen Taekman, and co.

Ramona Singer’s 2023 net worth

Ramona burst onto the Bravo scene all the way back in 2008. Since then, she’s launched businesses, raised her daughter, and gone through a divorce.

Despite going through all kinds of ups and downs in her life, Ramona’s 2023 net worth stands at a hefty $16 million, reports Celebrity Net Worth.

She lived in New York’s Upper East Side during her early years on The Real Housewives of New York City. And she also owns a home in the Hamptons worth an estimated $6.43 million, writes The New York Post.

Ramona’s career

When fans first met Ramona on RHONY season 1, she explained how important her career was to her so she could maintain her “financial independence.”

The “sexy mom” had a background in fashion and marketing and previously worked as a buyer.

Speaking on the show, she explained she bought “excess inventory such as shoes and clothes and resells it to major and minor discount stores.”

Ramona later went on to have a career as a real estate agent and she also launched a skincare line called Ageless By Ramona. She earned $500,000 per season she appeared on RHONY.

Her ex-husband, Mario Singer, is a businessman. In season 1, Ramona revealed he worked at his third-generation family business making religious articles.

RHUGT star’s divorce

As Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip arrives on Peacock this December, singleton Dorinda Medley isn’t the only lady looking for romance.

When asked about her current relationship status on RHUGT, Ramona says: “I have whatever I want. I’m very pleased.”

After marrying in 1992, Ramona and Mario went their separate ways, and their divorce was finalized in 2016.

Arguing with Bethenny Frankel years back on RHONY, Ramona said: “Don’t attack my business, because I support my family. I gave half my money to my husband that I was married to for 30 years. My heart was cut out.”

Despite Ramona’s comment, a 2016 report from Bustle states: “…it appears that the two have split their assets right down the middle.”

She kept the family’s Hamptons home but later sold her New York apartment.

According to Reality Blurb, Ramona opted to rent out her Hamptons home for $165,000 per month in 2018.

Celebrity Net Worth estimates Mario’s net worth slightly higher than Ramona’s at $18 million.
