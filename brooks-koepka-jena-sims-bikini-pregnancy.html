Brooks Koepka's wife Jena Sims stuns in neon bikini after announcing pregnancy

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

JENA Sims flaunts her tan as she plays ball in the Californian sunshine.

The American model and actress, who stunned in a neon-coloured swimsuit, is usually seen at the side-lines of golf tournaments supporting her pro golfer husband Brooks Koepka.

After announcing their pregnancy in a sweet bump shot, Koepka then accidentally revealed during an interview the 34-year-old is pregnant with a baby boy.

Discussing what traits he hopes the new arrival will inherit from him, the four-time major winner, 33, quipped: “I hope he gets a lot more traits from my wife than he does me”.

Jena recently recounted her Sports Illustrated “dream”.

She competed in the Miss Teen USA beauty pageant in 2007, but failed to place, and has since went on to become an actress and a model.

Films she has starred in include the lead role in Attack of the 50 foot Cheerleader, a comedy horror movie filmed in 2012.

She also appeared in Three-headed Shark Attack, in which a mutated great white kills former WWE champion Rob Van Dam.

Her other roles include Sharknado 5: Global Swarming, and TV shows, Entourage, Dexter and One Tree Hill.

Away from the big screen Sims runs her own non-profit organisation called the 'Pageant of Hope' - which hosts beauty pageants for children with cancer and other disadvantages.

Meanwhile, since his move to the LIV Golf League, Koepka has seen his net worth swell.

The two-time US Open and PGA Championship winner is now worth a staggering £40million, according to reports.

Koepka also has sponsorship partnerships with Srixon, Rolex, Netjets, as well brands Nike and Michelob Ultra.

As with many golfers, Koepka has made the very exclusive Jupiter Island his home.

The former world No1 spent £2.5million on a stunning four bedroom, five-and-a-half bathroom property on the periphery of Jupiter Island in Florida.

The 3,900-square-foot compound was built in 1993, but Koepka spent some of his £25million earnings renovating it.

The Florida-born star completely replaced the swimming pool - making it bigger by using up more of the grounds.

Koepka also rebuilt the boat dock and installed a new fence for greater privacy.

He also loves his motors, and boasts a £360,000 Rolls-Royce in his collection.
