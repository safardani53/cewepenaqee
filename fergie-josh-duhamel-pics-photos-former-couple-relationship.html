See Photos Of The Former Couples Relationship Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 17, 2024

Fergie and Josh Duhamel attend Trevor Project Live in Los Angeles, on Dec. 8, 2013. The couple met in 2004 when her group the Black Eyed Peas appeared on his NBC show ‘Las Vegas.’ He was instantly struck with how sexy the singer was and they began dating. Three years later in Dec. of 2007, Josh popped the question and the couple got engaged. They married on Jan. 10, 2009. The couple have one child, son Axl Jack who was born in August of 2013. The pair announced their separation in Sept. of 2017, even though they had secretly parted ways earlier in the year. Fergie finally filed for divorce on May 31, 2019 to bring their union to an end. The couple maintains an amicable relationship and happily co-parent Axl. Josh has said in interviews that he would like to find a woman that he can have more children with because he loves being a dad so much and wants a sibling for Axl.
