Why is Nottingham Forest vs Sheffield United NOT on TV tonight?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

NOTTINGHAM Forest take on Sheffield United in the Premier League tonight.

The game, however, will NOT be shown on TV, much to fans' disappointment.

Forest kicked off their domestic campaign with a 2-1 defeat to Arsenal while Sheffield United lost 1-0 to Crystal Palace last weekend.

The last time Forest faced the Blades was in the 2022 Championship play-off semi-final, which Steve Cooper's men won on penalties.

The Blades will be out for revenge, but if you want to watch the game, you're going to have to get your hands on a ticket.

Why is Forest vs Sheff Utd not on TV?

Forest vs Sheff Utd won't be shown on TV because the game was originally supposed to take place on a different date.

The game was scheduled for Saturday, August 19, but had to moved to tonight instead.

That's because in the cricket, the Trent Rockets are up against Birmingham Phoenix in the The Hundred on Saturday night.

Due to security fears and potential heavy congestion in the city, the decision was taken to move the Premier League game.

And although Sky Sports usually show Friday night games, the fixture wasn't scheduled in with the broadcaster, therefore the game won't be on TV.
