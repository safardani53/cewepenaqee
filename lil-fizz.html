Lil' Fizz Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Lil' Fizz whose real name is Dreux Pierre is a rapper and an actor from the United States. He is mainly popular for being the rapper of an American R&B boy band B2K. He is also famous for being a Songwriter, Record Producer, and reality TV star.

Early life

Lil' Fizz is also known by the name Fizzo and he was born as Dreux Pierre Frederic on 26th November 1985 in New Orleans, Louisiana, the United States of America to African and American parents. He has Louisiana Creole origin and his nationality is American.

Fizz has one sister named Brooke. His height is five feet and seven inches tall and he has black ethnicity.

Personal life

He was once in love with Moniece Slaughter. From this romantic love life, Moniece Slaughter became pregnant for the first time in her life and she finally gave birth to their first child together with a son Kamron David Frédéric in early 2010. Kamron David Frédéric is now aged 6.

Career and net worth

Lil' became interested in music and rap since he was a boy. With the increase of his height and age, his dreams of music also propped up, and then in 2001, he joined an American R&B boy band B2K. This brand has been active since 1998.

Their second album was released on the 29th of October 2002. Within a short period of time, they released four albums, and this band disbanded in 2004.

After that, he began pursuing his solo career. He has also been active as an actor since 2002. In 2004, he appeared in the movie , starring Omarion, Steve Harvey, and Meagan Good, where he portrayed the role of Rashaun.

Besides that, he has also acted in alongside Anthony Anderson and Chico Benymon, and in the TV series , featuring Rami Malek.

Net worth

His net worth is $2 million. Fizz made most of his money from the sales of his record albums whose earnings are currently unknown.
