Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 2, 2024

Fans slam Heidi and Sofia over harsh treatment of contestant

Fans were absolutely appalled at the clip, taking to social media to slam both Heidi and Sofia for their harsh treatment of the nervous contestant.

"I did not find this funny, I found this to be offensive to those with neurological disabilities," one ripped.

"So everyone is gonna ignore what Heidi said tonight ? The devil deserves a 2nd chance ? IS SHE FOR REALLLLLLL RIGHT NOW. Unbelievable," a second fumed.

"I thought this was actually really mean. you can tell he was nervous," a third claimed.

Contestant annoys judges, conclusion

The performer strung one chord, and then looked down at his pockets once more, saying: "I'm sorry, I just gotta tune up real quick."

But the pause was enough to push Heidi over the edge as she slammed her hand down on the button.

The crowd cheered as the supermodel slammed the contestant, saying: "Are you kidding me Sunny?"

"I just wanted everyone to be a part of it," Sunny explained, looking downtrodden at his fate.

But the judges showed no sympathy, as Sofia yelled at the stage: "Goodbye!" with an angry expression.

Contestant annoys judges, part three

Finally, the contestant relented, as he promised: "Okay, I'll sing. Okay, I'll sing. I'm gonna sing."

Simon replied: "Okay, put the things down and sing please."

Sunny said: "Okay, I'm sorry," as he hid his toy shakers in his pockets.

"Hand is traveling, it's getting closer Sunny," Heidi threatened again, moving her arm just inches above the buzzer.

"Sunny! Tick tock tick tock."

Sunny answered: "Okay! I'm gonna sing," as he picked up his ukulele and looked at the strings.

"Good, thank god," Simon replied.
