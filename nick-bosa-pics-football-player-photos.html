Pics Of The Football Player Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 19, 2024

It seems as if Nick Bosa’s destiny was to play in the NFL. The Ohio State Buckeyes defensive end is the son of former NFL player, John Bosa. His uncle is his father’s former Miami Dolphins teammate, Eric Kumerow. His brother is Joey Bosa, who was drafted by the Los Angeles Chargers in 2016. It seems that Nick was meant to play football, and in 2019, his fate will be decided.

With the Buckeyes, Nick was a defensive beast, racking up 29 tackles in his true freshman year. He recorded 34 as a sophomore in 2017, with 8.5 sacks. An injury in 2018 ended not just his football season but his career at Ohio State, as he withdrew from the university in order to avoid any further injuries that would jeopardize his NFL career. However, Nick’s social media habits – as he’s a pro-Donald Trump, anti-Colin Kaepernick tweeter – might keep him out of the Overall No. 1 draft spot.
