Photos Of The Director &amp; His Wife Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 15, 2024

Director Quentin Tarantino and wife Daniella Pick pose on the red carpet for the Lifetime Achievement Award ceremony at the 16th edition of the Rome Film Fest in Rome, Italy, Oct. 2021. 

Quentin Tarantino has made name for himself in Hollywood with films like Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Kill Bill, Pulp Fiction, and more, winning numerous awards and accolades for his work. Perhaps his biggest award, however, is his wife Daniella Pick, an Israeli singer. 

Before he and Daniella walked down the aisle, the writer-director stated that when he was working on a movie, it was “all about the movie,” causing him to put off the prospect of settling down with a wife and kids. “Nothing can get in my way,” he said at the time. After the pair briefly dated in 2009, they eventually came back together in 2016, and Quentin seemed to change his tune as the duo ended up tying the knot in a goregeous intimate ceremony at their Beverly Hills home in 2018. 

The happy couple then announced they were having a baby in 2019. Their baby boy, Leo, was born Feb. 22, 2020 and named after Daniella’s maternal grandmother. In early 2022, they confirmed they were welcoming a second child to their family. As of Jan. 2020, the couple announced they would be splitting their time between Tel Aviv, Israel, and Los Angeles.
