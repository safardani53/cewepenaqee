MAC Cosmetics X Mariah Carey Makeup to Launch Holiday 2016

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Mimi does makeup! Just in time for the most wonderful time of the year, elusive chanteuse Mariah Carey (whose reality show premieres December 4 on E!) is giving Us all a gift: a 23-piece makeup collection for MAC Cosmetics. Just some of the items in the line include five lipsticks (each $20), five lip glosses ($18.50), two eyeshadow palettes ($41) as well as the diva’s signature false eyelash strips, liquid eyeliner and shimmering golden powders. 

But the best part? Products are housed in glam glitter packaging, obviously. Grab the sure-to-be-a-platinum-hit Butterfly Bling Lipglass, a pinky bronze shimmer gloss, that’s an online-only exclusive when the collection launches at maccosmetics.com December 8. The full collection hits MAC shops and department-store counters nationwide December 15 and sticks around only through January 26, 2017.

Editor’s picks (clockwise from top left): Lash 05 ($18), Liquid Eye Liner ($22), I’m That Chick You Like/Eye Shadow X4 ($41), I Get So OOO Lipstick ($20), Dreamlover Lipglass ($18.50), and Pro Longwear Lip Pencil in So Dramatique (left) and New Ombré ($22 each)

Now, this isn’t Carey’s first trip to the beauty rodeo. She created an exclusive lipstick shade for MAC last holiday season, called “All I Want” and at the time, teased this collection with the caption: “It’s a major lip #moment! “All I Want” lipstick with @MACcosmetics is coming Dec. 2015! Plus, a full Mariah x MAC Beauty Icon collections coming later in 2016! #MACMariahCarey.” And, of course, the “Visions of Love” singer, who has 5-year-old twins with ex Nick Cannon, has a super popular perfume line including her newest scent, Dreams, and Luscious Pink and M, available at Kohl’s stores and kohls.com.

Plus, she’s in good company! The songstress joins some bold names who’ve done collaborations with MAC in 2016: Caitlyn Jenner, Taraji P. Henson and Ariana Grande, whose collab for the Viva Glam MAC Aids Fund is out now.
