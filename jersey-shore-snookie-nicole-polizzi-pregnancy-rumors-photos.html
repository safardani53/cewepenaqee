Although fans were supportive of Snooki's mom life, the reality television star has sparked speculation over a potential pregnancy.

'LET'S GO'

Fans think Snooki even dropped a hint in her Instagram Story earlier this week teasing baby number four.

The Jersey Shore star shared a text post to her Instagram Story about her astrological sign, Sagittarius.

Her horoscope promised that 2024 would be "very rich for you in many ways."

It read: "This year is very rich for you in many ways, and the future is on your side. We're not talking about the future as future tense or as a future time, but the future as a state of being – a futuristic way of thinking & living life.

"The future will come to you. You will also be restoring yourself in many ways. You will be restoring the way you experience joy and pleasure."

Snooki captioned her post: "LET'S GO."

She married Jionni LaValle in 2014 and the pair share three children, Lorenzo Dominic, Angelo James, and Giovanna Marie.

In addition to the post, fans have noticed that the MTV personality has been covering up wearing baggy clothing.

She's been spotted in several snaps wearing increasingly baggy tops.

Some are speculating she's trying to hide a baby bump, though she has yet to admit she's expecting.

BIG REVEAL

Earlier this month, Snooki unveiled another member of her family in an emotional post amid pregnancy rumors.

The TV personality posted a teaser about her rumored "fourth child" on social media, snapping a picture with a dog.

She knelt next to a poodle mix and tucked the dog under her chin.

Snooki gave a coy smile to the camera, cuddling the four-legged friend.

She captioned the post: "MY ROCKY! I've been a mess these last few days with my 4th child undergoing surgery!!

"I want to say thank you so much to @stfrancisvmc for taking care of my baby!! So grateful Rocky has the best care!"

She concluded: "BRB, going to cuddle & spoil him all day!"

GOT A CRAVING?

Before the post, Snooki shared another that led to even more speculation from fans.

She and her BFF Jenni "JWoww" Farley appeared in a pizza advertisement together.

Snooki uploaded a video featuring their respective children to her Instagram feed.

In it, fans noticed that she was wearing a dramatically oversized sweater that once again hid her stomach from view.

A fan asked: "Is she pregnant? Did I miss something?"
