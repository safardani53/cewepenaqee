Katt Williams Loses A Fight With A 7th Grader &amp; Twitter Roasts Him Alive Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 27, 2024

There are so many questions that need answers: Why was Katt Williams hanging out with teenagers? Why did he think it was okay to sucker punch some random kid? Most importantly, what is Katt going to do now that the entire world saw him get choked out by an alleged 7th grader (who turns out to be a wrestler)?

You better believe Twitter was having a blast at Katt’s failed audition to the UFC. Some said that the “transition from…sucker punching that kid to getting choked out” was absolutely hilarious. One Twitter user sarcastically suggested that getting beaten down by a teenager is a totally normal, everyday occurrence. It happens to everyone, right? Sadly, Katt is now reportedly the subject of a police investigation over this fight! The teen even claims Katt told him to throw the first punch!

Others were worried about Katt, saying that picking fights with kids is a quick way to ruin his career. Some even pointed out that Katt is “sick,” and that this behavior is “not a joke anymore.” Still, everyone mostly mocked Katt over this latest humiliation.
