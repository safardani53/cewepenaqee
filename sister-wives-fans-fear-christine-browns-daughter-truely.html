Sister Wives fans fear for Christine Brown's daughter Truely, 13, after teen makes 'concerning' thre

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

SISTER Wives fans have shared their concerns for Christine Brown's youngest daughter Truely after the teen made a worrisome threat in a new video.

The TLC star has been updating her followers as she settles into a new home with her fiancé David Woolley.

On Tuesday, Christine, 51, took to Instagram to show fans her backyard renovation project in a new video.

The TV personality's kids filmed as she led fans out the back door of her home and onto her patio.

Christine showed off her thinner-than-ever figure in a pair of baggy black sweatpants and an oversized gray shirt with a floral design.

The TLC star paired the look with black-rimmed glasses and black ballet flats as she explained her vision for the backyard.

"This is the new deck and our view, except its huge. The deck used to be very, very small and now there's stairs," she explained.

But as Christine began leading fans further down onto the grass, her 13-year-old daughter - who she shares with her ex-husband Kody Brown - could be heard making a shocking statement.

"Mom, you do realize that at this point I could sneak out of my window at night," she asserted.

The Sister Wives star was shocked, turning toward her teen to show a face of utter confusion.

Christine's older daughter who was filming the video snapped in response: "Oh my gosh, Truely!" but the family matriarch just laughed the tense moment off.

She then continued on to show more construction being done on her Utah property, including a brand new hot tub, shed, playground for her grandkids, firepit, and garden boxes.

"Isn't it pretty? It looks beautiful," she gushed.

The mother of six captioned her post: "I’m getting my backyard ready for grandkids to come play," as fans filled the comments with praise.

Christine shares her children with her ex-Kody Brown: Aspyn, 28; Mykelti, 26; Paedon, 25; Gwendlyn, 21; Ysabel, 19; and Truely, 13.

DREAM WEDDING

The U.S. Sun previously reported that she's planning on having her dream wedding to her fiancé David this summer, probably in July, before the kids go back to school in the fall.

A source claimed the nuptials would occur in Utah, where the newly engaged couple recently bought a new $770K home.

"It's going to be, from what I hear, really intimate and mostly close family," the source dished.

"She's super close to her mom and obviously her kids will be there and his kids and their families."

DROPPING POUNDS

Christine has been focused on her diet and exercise regimen as she hopes to shed 20 pounds before the big day.

"She works out every day. She's like super dedicated to the 'hot mom' comeback thing," a Sister Wives source told The U.S. Sun.

When asked how much she's looking to lose, the insider revealed: "Oh, probably 20 more pounds.

"She's happy with how far she's come and David thinks she's perfect."

FOLLOWER MOTIVATION

The mom of six has made it known that she will be starting a six-week challenge with her Plexus followers.

Christine has been promoting the upcoming weight loss challenge on her Instagram, asking her fans to message her if they want to sign up.

She captioned a recent post: "I get asked quite often - how did you lose all your weight? This little drink right here with a few others.

"Google gut health & weight loss - you will see how it's ALL connected. It's pretty fascinating, actually. So I jumped on board and have been consistent since.

"I choose these products because they get to the root of the issue, aka gut health = weight loss and better living."
