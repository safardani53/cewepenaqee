GMA's Ginger Zee admits she 'borrowed' outfit from co-host as she shows off sexy white suit live on-

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 29, 2024

GOOD Morning America star Ginger Zee has taken to social media to give her co-star Eva Pilgrim props for loaning her a fashion look.

Ginger announced on social media that Eva allowed her to sport one of her ensembles when she co-hosted GMA on Tuesday.

The chic outfit that Ginger, 42, wore was an all-white pantsuit that fabulously framed her fit figure, providing a hint of room since it wasn't so skintight.

Her pants were flared, flowing stylishly over her pointy-toed heels.

Underneath her slightly oversized blazer jacket was a cloud-colored cropped suit vest that exposed her navel.

The TV personality accessorized her sleek suit with a layered necklace and some rings.

Ginger took to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday to share a snap of her co-anchoring on GMA in the suit.

The ABC host made sure to shout out Eva, 40, in her caption since that's who she burrowed the outfit from.

"In my Eva Pilgrim suit (borrowed it) this earth week," Ginger wrote.

Ginger definitely got her wear out of Eva's fashion piece.

DOUBLE TAKE

That same day, the famous meteorologist visited with the hosts of The View in honor of Earth Day coming up, wearing the entire suit minus the jacket.

Ahead of her appearance on Tuesday, Ginger shared a mirror selfie from backstage as she prepared for the start of her View segment.

She was all smiles as she showed off her strong arms and toned tummy in a white vest crop top and matching pants combo.

Ginger captioned the shot on her Instagram feed: “Taking over the guest room here at The View to talk Power Of Water for earth week!”

Ginger posted the snap to her IG Story, as well, and told fans: “See you on The View!"

After watching Ginger on the talk show, fans couldn’t help but gush about both her outfit and the information she shared on the show.

“Love your outfit Ginger!” one person praised.

Another insisted that she resembled a beloved Big Bang Theory actress.

The Twitter user shared: “Ginger Zee and Kaley Cuoco look alike! Like twins!!”

“Let it be known, Ginger Zee is my girl,” a third viewer tweeted after seeing Ginger on the talk show. “She is the coolest! She is intelligent! Clap it up!”
