Lily Collins stuns in a nude bikini after Golden Globe nomination for Emily in Paris

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 12, 2024

EMILY in Paris star Lily Collins keeps everything crossed as she hopes for a major award.

The actress, 31, is up for a Best Actress in a TV Series gong at next week’s Golden Globes for her lead role in the Netflix comedy-drama.

British-born Emily, daughter of Genesis rocker Phil, 70, plays an American marketing exec who relocates to France.

She will duke it out with stars including The Big Bang Theory’s Kaley Cuoco, 35, who is nominated for mystery series The Flight Attendant, and Schitt’s Creek’s Catherine O’Hara, 66, for the gong.

She has to Francais her chances.

The show stayed in the Top 10 on the streaming platform for weeks after its early October release, and it was renewed for a Season 2.

It comes as Golden Globes fans are slamming the award show for giving nominations to The Prom’s James Corden and Lily Collins.

The actors are being mocked for receiving nods, despite their “mediocre” and “lazy” performances.

One user summed up why many were upset, writing: “I enjoyed watching ‘Emily in Paris,’ but does it deserve a golden globe nomination?? absolutely not, i’ve enjoyed plenty of things that are absolutely horrible.”
