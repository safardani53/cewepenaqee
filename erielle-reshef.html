Erielle Reshef Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 29, 2024

Erielle Reshef is an American news anchor who has won Emmy Award for her talent, confidence, and skills in presenting news. She has also worked in the Oklahoma City News Channel 5. She is currently serving ABC News.

Early Life, Family & Education

Erielle Reshef was born on September 23, 1983, in Oklahoma, the United States of America, in a Jewish family.

Her father, Dr. Eli Reshef, is a Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility expert while her mother, Edie S. Roodman is the director of the Jewish Federation of Greater Oklahoma City.

She has two siblings, Evans Reshef and Eitan Reshef. She is of American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity.

Erielle has attended the Westminster School in Simsbury, Hartford County, Connecticut. She later attended Indiana University from where she graduated with a degree in Political Science and Jewish studies.
