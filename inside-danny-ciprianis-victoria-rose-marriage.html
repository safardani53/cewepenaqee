Inside Danny Ciprianis three-year marriage to Victoria Rose - and reason why their relation

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 25, 2024

DANNY Cipriani and Victoria Rose had been married for three years until their relationship crumbled in recent weeks.

The former England rugby star, 36, and his wife first started dating in April 2020 - often sharing sweet snaps of themselves online.

But their love story has now come to an end - here's why.

Danny and Victoria, 42, first shacked up in April 2020 and got engaged after just four months later.

The following year the couple tied the knot in a sweet yet small ceremony.

At the time Victoria, a healthcare campaigner, posted on Instagram: "Today we officially became Mr & Mrs Cipriani in a small intimate setting with our nearest and dearest.

"We are looking forward to our big day Caribbean style when travel is permitted.

"Cheers to love, laughter, but especially to our happily ever after. Our adventure begins. Dannie Cipriani I love you."

Danny himself wrote: “Officially Mrs Cipriani @victoriarosecipriani - married my best friend today.”

The loved-up couple often snapped sweet selfies cuddled up together.

They could be seen with their arms around one another at home, at bars and on holiday.

The pair had seemed happy - until earlier this year.

Fans started to notice their marriage wasn't as strong as it initially had been, as they unfollowed each other on social media.

Danny had also slowed his posting of Victoria - and hadn't put any photos or stories up in recent months.

Fans also noticed the 42-year-old mum seemed to have removed her wedding ring.

Then this week it was rumoured the couple has split - before they confirmed it themselves.

All was said to have come crashing down after Victoria saw messages on Danny's phone.

A source told Mail Online: "Victoria had been increasingly unhappy over the last few months.

"Things got tense and then she saw some text messages on his phone and it led to some difficult rows between them and they have now split up.

"They are both devastated at the breakdown of their marriage."

'FRAGILE'

Danny is now said to have moved out of the home they shared in Kent.

Taking to social media, the 36-year-old said: "Victoria and I sat together this weekend and agreed to separate after 4 years of marriage.

"Whilst we've had some wonderful times together, we've also recognised we're on different journeys and whilst this is sad news, it's also a new chapter for us both to pursue our own happiness.

"I only wish the best for Victoria and her children."

But the mum clearly wasn't happy with Danny taking it upon himself to share their private affairs on social media.

She quickly made a post of her own - slamming the rugby player and asking people to leave her alone during this "fragile" time.

Hitting back, Danny wrote: "I'm not sure why Daniel needs to tell Instagram of his relationship status. I don't wish for the invasion.

"Could I ask for some privacy, while navigating through this fragile time."

'AWFUL FOR VIC'

But just two days later, Danny was spotted working out with a model.

Female boxer Isa Yasmijn posted a video on her Instagram stories showing her sparring with the former England rugby star alone in a "living room session".

A source told The Sun: “It is obviously awful for Vic to see this kind of thing posted publicly online.

"They aren’t even divorced yet. It’s been a matter of days since he announced to the world they have split and he looks like he is in this woman's living room.

"The whole thing is being handled so disrespectfully.”

There is no suggestion Isa is connected to Cipriani’s marriage split with ex-wife Victoria but she’s the first woman he’s been pictured with since news broke of it and the pair follow each other on social media platform Instagram.

Asked if she and Danny were in a relationship, she said: "I am not interested in answering your questions."
