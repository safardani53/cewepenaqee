Who is Maya Hawke?

Maya Hawke is an American actress and model. Maya was born in New York City and was charmed by acting.

Like both her mother and grandmother, Hawke modeled for Vogue at the start of her career.

She was also chosen as the face of the British fashion retailer All Saints’ 2016/2017 collection. Her recent project includes the film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.

Early Life of Maya Hawke 

Maya Hawke was born on 8 July 1998. She is 24 years old as of the year 2022. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.

She was born and brought up in a well-settled family in New York City, New York, United States. She is an American by nationality and belongs to the white ethnic group.

Maya Hawke is a believer in Buddhism and is fascinated by the life of Gautama Buddha.

Maya Hawke’s parents are Ethan Hawke and Uma Thurman. Ethan Hawke, her father, is a popular writer, actor, and director in the American film industry. Uma Thurman, her mother, is a writer, actress, producer, and model by profession.

She also has four siblings, her younger brother’s name is Levon Roan Thurman-Hawke. She also has two half-sisters from her father’s second marriage and another half-sister from her mother’s ex-fiance.

Her stepbrother’s name is Ryan Shawhughes.

She completed her early schooling at Saint Ann’s School in Brooklyn, New York City, United States. Before that, she enrolled herself at various schools as she keeps on changing schools due to dyslexia.

After that, she also enrolled herself at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts in London and Stella Adler Studio of Acting in New York. She also attended The Juilliard School in New York City from where she completed her graduation.

Maya Hawke Career

 

Maya Hawke started her career as a model with the popular American fashion magazine Vogue following in the footsteps of her mother and grandmother.

After that, she has completed various modeling assignments and has modeled for many famous fashion brands like All Saints, Calvin Klein, and others.

In the year 2017, Maya Hawke made her acting debut with her appearance in the popular movie Little Women in which she played the lead role of Jo March.

After that, she made her appearance in many popular movies like Ladyworld, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Human Capital, and many other popular movies.

The biggest breakthrough in her career came out when she made her appearance in the popular Netflix series Stranger Things in the third season in the year 2019. She played the role of Robin in the show and mostly shared the screen space with Joe Kerry’s character of Steve Harrington.

Along with her acting career in the year 2019, she started her singing career and released two singles titled To Love a Boy and Stay Open.

After that in the year 2020, she gave her first solo live performance in New York City. On 18 March 2020, she released her debut album titled Blush along with a single titled By Myself.
