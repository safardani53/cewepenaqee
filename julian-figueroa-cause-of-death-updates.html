Julin Figueroa death updates Actress Maribel Guardia mourns son as fans show support for widow

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 23, 2024

Fans comment on star's obituary

Fans of Julián Figueroa have taken to his obituary on Cleveland.com to honor the late singer.

One person wrote: "An incredibly talented singer, that had so more music to sing. Heartfelt sympathies to the Figueroa family, so very sorry for your loss, God be with you all. Psalms 23."

Another said: "I offer my deepest sympathies to the family of Julian Figueroa. Such a great loss so soon. May the God who gives comfort be with you during this difficult time."

Some of Julián's most popular songs

The Los Angeles Times shared a list of five of Julián Figueroa's most popular songs, which include:

  • Ay Amor
  • Yo sería
  • Volaré
  • 25 rosas
  • Un idiota

Previous acting appearances

Previously, Julián Figueroa appeared on film in the TelevisaUnivision soap "Mi Camino es Amarte," as Leonardo Santos, a young man from a family of truck drivers who aspired to become a well-known singer.

Gabriel Soto, Susana González, and Sara Corrales were among the other cast members.

Broadcasting ended in March.

Film's executive producers pay tribute

The executive producers of "Centurion: The Dancing Stallion," George Gamble and Nancy White Gamble, were among the countless actors and musicians who sent their condolences to Costa Rican actress Maribel Guardia following the unexpected loss of her son.

“We are honored to have seen Julián’s magnificent talents firsthand: songwriting, singing and acting,” the couple wrote.

“He was kind, affectionate, a loving son, father, and loving friend; he was a true gentleman. We were exceptionally proud of his work on behalf of our film, and even more proud of the good man he was.”
