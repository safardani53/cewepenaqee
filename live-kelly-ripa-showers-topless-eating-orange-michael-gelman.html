LIVE's Kelly Ripa has gone topless in the shower in an NSFW video as she tests out how eating oranges while in there can be beneficial.

On Tuesday's episode of Live, Kelly mentioned she had read about the benefits of eating oranges while showering.

"I read this article about why you should eat oranges in the shower," she attested during the beginning of the show.

"I found it fascinating and weird and we tried it," she said..

"They are saying that when you eat oranges in the shower, it's the benefit of the scene, the aromatherapy of it all, which I think can probably be achieved by essential oils," Kelly explained of what she had read.

She continued: "They're saying another practical reason in the shower is so that the juice doesn't get everywhere."

LETTING RIP-A

Meanwhile, Kelly lashed out at her husband live-on-air after the couple argued over their home life.

On Monday's episode of Live with Kelly and Mark, the duo seemingly got derailed after disagreeing about a small aspect of their private life.

In the argument, Kelly, 52, and her husband Mark Consuelos, 52, debated who watched social media videos loudly at home.

Mark said: "If it has video and it's moving, it has volume on it.

His wife clapped back: "How do I know it has video and it's moving?"

TV and film star Mark responded: "As our son says, you're capping."

After Kelly said that she didn't know what capping means, Mark sassed: "It means you're lying."

Off-screen, Michael Gelman - the daytime TV show's executive producer - shouted: "I'm getting the therapist back. You need another session."

After Michael, Mark, and the audience started laughing, Kelly claimed: "This is, I believe, gaslighting. I'm getting gaslit."

GOODBYE FOR NOW!

This week, The U.S Sun exclusively learned that Live was taking a break from filming until the fall.

Ahead of making a splash with their new show opener, Kelly, Mark, and the show's crew will be able to enjoy the rest of their summer away from the set.

The group will reunite to film new episodes on September 5; the same time last year that Live with Kelly and Ryan - with ex-host Ryan Seacrest, 48 - premiered its new season.

A source close to production told The U.S. Sun: "The Live family is taking their annual summer break, but will still be providing fresh new material to viewers at home during this time."

The insider added that even though there is no filming happening over the next few weeks, there are still some staffers hard at work planning Halloween content.
