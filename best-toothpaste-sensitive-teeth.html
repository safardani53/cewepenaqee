6 Best Toothpastes For Sensitive Teeth 2020 | The Sun UK

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 28, 2024

TOOTH sensitivity can be really painful and uncomfortable, whether you feel that jolt when you bite into something cold or get a twinge with that first sip of your morning coffee.

While there are many reasons that could explain your sensitive teeth, there is one helpful, at-home fix: finding the best toothpaste for sensitive teeth, which can also work to strengthen enamel and soothe sore gums.

Typically, sensitive teeth are the result of exposed dentin, which can happen when the tooth's protective enamel becomes damaged or when gums recede.

This can occur after a dental treatment, like a filling, or it can be caused by decay or a cracked tooth.

Other reasons for sensitivity may be overbrushing, too much teeth grinding, and diseases like gingivitis. If you're keen to find out more about toothache and tooth decay, check out the NHS website.

Brushing your teeth with a fluoride toothpaste isn't just part of a healthy teeth and gum regime - the right toothpaste can help protect teeth as you clean.

Favourite brands like Colgate and Oral-B all have sensitive teeth toothpastes, while Sensodyne is widely considered the leading toothpaste brand for sensitive teeth, whether you're also looking to whiten teeth or to help improve gum soreness.
