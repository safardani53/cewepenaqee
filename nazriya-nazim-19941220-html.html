Nazriya Nazim (Movie Actress) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, February 26, 2024

Nazriya Nazim is an Indian actress and producer who mainly works in Malayalam and Tamil films. She started her career as a host on Asianet Malayalam TV channel before pursuing an acting career. She made her debut as a child artist with Palunku, then as a lead actress in the 2013 Malayalam film Maad Dad. She went on to star in blockbuster films such as Neram, Raja Rani, Ohm Shanthi Oshaana and Bangalore Days. After her marriage to actor Fahadh Faasil, she stopped acting. She made her comeback in 2018 with Anjali Menon's film Koode.
