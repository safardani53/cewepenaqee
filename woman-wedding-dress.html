I thought I found the perfect wedding guest dress but everyones said the same thing - now I'm dou

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 18, 2024

A WOMAN who thought she'd found the perfect dress to wear to a wedding has hit back at people saying it's "too white" for such an occasion.

Lauren took to TikTok to share a video of herself sporting the ASOS gown, which is described as "sage green" on the website.

"Quite possibly the most beautiful wedding guest dress ever," she wrote over the top of the video.

"ASOS, this is a dream," she captioned the clip.

But tonnes of people in the comments section insisted that the dress looked too white for her to wear to a wedding.

"If there’s a chance that it looks white on camera it’s a no go!" one wrote.

"I *know* it’s sage green, but it’s baaaassssicalllyyyy white," another added.

With Lauren replying: "Just stay with me… I posted the colour in a new video if it’s white there then I will accept my error but I swear it’s green!"

"Too close to white," someone else wrote.

"If the light you have on in your room is enough to make it this light it will probably show up white in the pictures/outside."
