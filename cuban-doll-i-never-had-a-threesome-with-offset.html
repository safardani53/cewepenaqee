I Never Had a Threesome with Offset!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Cuban Doll would like to be excluded from the Cardi B and Offset narrative.

Please and thank you.

The little-known rapper (whose real name is NOT Cuban Doll, we just learned) has found herself at the center of a brewing sex scandal in the wake of Cardi B and Offset’s split.

The break-up was confirmed this week by Cardi on video, who said she and her husband of about a year simply fell out of love and will remain "friends and business partners."

She did not appear to have any hard feelings against anyone upon announcing this predictable news.

BUT THEN… TMZ posted text messages between Offset and someone named Summer Bunni (also, not her real name) that seemed to depict the artist asking this random woman to have a threesome.

He wanted this threesome to include himself, Bunni and Cuban Doll.

What about Cardi B?

She was very pregnant at the time and actually gave birth just a few weeks after this exchange allegedly took place, in July of 2018.

Yikes, right? Only Tristan Thompson would be proud of Offset for doing such a thing.

Did this threesome actually take place, though?

Cuban Doll says she. In fact, she tells TMZ that she’s never even met the now-former Mr. Cardi B.

"I don’t know him. I never spoke to him. I never met him," Doll told TMZ yesterday when stopped and asked about the situation, adding:

"I’m just caught in the middle. It’s all based on [Summer Bunni] trying to get clout. It’s all some clout-getting stuff."

Cuban says that she and Bunni used to be friends, but had some kind of falling out and have not been close for awhile.

It remains unclear whether Bunni provided TMZ with the text message above, but Summer has since tried to apologize for her role in Cardi and Offset’s separation.

The Instagram user basically admitted on Wednesday that she was screwing around with Offset on her own at least, but stopped doing so after he and Cardi welcomed their daughter.

"I never wanted to break up a happy home or be a cause of someone just saying, like, ’F—k it,’ to get a divorce or anything like that," she adds, explaining that she didn’t think the marriage was very serious.

(We’re pretty sure that view is antithetical to the entire defintion of marriage, but whatevs.)

Neither Cuban Doll nor Summer Bunni have talked to Cardi B about this gigantic mess.

Doll told TMZ, however, that the superstar singer knows she had "nothing to do" with Offset, while Bunni has confessed to the opposite.

She totally did Offset.

But she’s very sorry for it.

Bunni penned the message below on Instagram in response…

… and she says she would like Cardi to know the following:

I would like her to know that I’m a very genuine person and I’m not here to break up her family, or mess up what she has going on with her husband and I’m very sorry that this has happened.

I’m very sorry that I didn’t think about my worth as a young lady and I just hope that she can overcome this.

I know that her and Offset actually do have love for each other; I can tell.

So I just hope that, with this, she can just focus on her love for Offset.
