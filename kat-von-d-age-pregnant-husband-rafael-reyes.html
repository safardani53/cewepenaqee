Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 5, 2024

SINCE 2018, Mexican-American tattoo artist Kat Von D has been married to Rafael Reyes.

As she continues to make headlines, fans want to know more about her husband and their relationship.

Who is Kat Von D's husband, Rafael Reyes?

On February 21, 2018, Kat married Rafael.

Outside of being known as Kat's husband, he is known for his career as an author, artist, and musician.

As one-half of the electronic rock duo Prayers, Rafael, who is also credited as Leafar Seyer, is said to have created the cholo goth genre of music.

"I had to create my own language, my own truth, my own way of seeing myself in a world that wanted to punish me for no reason," he explained during an interview with Cryptic Rock.

"I relentlessly fought against my environment day and night without anybody there to love me. I won the fight because today we have CholoGoth!

"My story is embedded in those words spoken in my songs," he added.

While Reyes might have a high-profile career, additional information on his personal life is unclear because he often stays out of the spotlight.

What is Kat Von D's net worth?

Kat has been in the spotlight since the mid-2000s when she appeared on the TLC reality series, Miami Ink.

She was on the show from 2005 to 2007 but ultimately left after she had a falling out with fellow tattoo artist Ami James.

She went on to start her own series, LA Ink, which ran from 2007 to 2011 and was filmed at her studio, High Voltage Tattoo.

The success of her show, as well as her tattoo studio, has helped her amass a fortune.

As of October 2023, she has an estimated net worth of $20million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Does Kat Von D have any kids?

When Kat isn't on set or in her studio, she can often be found spending time with her family.

Since tying the knot with Rafael, the pair have welcomed one child, a son named Leafar Von D Reyes, who was born in December 2018.

"Meet our beautiful baby boy, Leafar Von D Reyes," Kat wrote in an Instagram post.

"Thank you to all our beloved friends+family, fans+followers for your patience in us announcing his long awaited arrival!"

At this time, details on Leafar's personal life are unclear due to his young age, but he often makes appearances on his mother's Instagram page.
