Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 25, 2024

AN ALDI shopper has found an affordable dupe for a popular children's toy.

Aldi is one of the most affordable grocery stores in the country, offering low prices on everything from food and home goods to clothing and toys.

The store offers its own versions of popular brand-name items that keep customers coming back for more.

On a recent shopping trip, Lisa from Drugstore Divas (@drugstoredivas) spotted a familiar toy for an unfamiliar price.

On X, previously known as Twitter, the shopper shared a picture of a box of Magne Builds magnetic building pieces.

The package came with 26 pieces that can be used to make 3D designs, and cost just $9.99.

"Next time you're in ALDI, look for the new Magne Builds sets. They're only $9.99 and there's a glitter and a glow in the dark," she wrote.

"They're comparable to the more expensive ones you can find online."

For comparison, a set of name-brand Magna-Tiles with 37 pieces costs $59.99 at Target.

The play set has rave reviews from shoppers on Reddit, who said it is "very worth it."
