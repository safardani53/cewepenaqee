At Midnight A Romantic Comedy Movie Is Set To Released On Paramount+

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 29, 2024

On Friday, February 10, 2023, a new romantic comedy named At Midnight will launch on Paramount+. The film tells the tale of two individuals from very different backgrounds and occupations who meet and form a friendship. The rom-com follows their connection as an inevitable romance blooms, affecting their lives. Diego Boneta and Monica Barbaro play the primary parts, with a number of others playing crucial supporting ones. Jonah Feingold directs the film, which he co-wrote with Maria Hinojos and Giovanni M. Porta.

At Midnight by Paramount+: Trailer, Plot, What to Expect, and More Details

On January 10, 2023, Paramount Plus published the official trailer for At Midnight, which depicts the lives of the film’s two main protagonists, Alejandro and Sophie. Alejandro is a hotel manager, and Sophie is a well-known Hollywood actress. The trailer depicts the two characters’ interactions briefly and emphasizes the disparity in their lifestyles. Alejandro and Sophie meet in the most uncomfortable and humorous circumstances, yet they finally bond and fall in love. Overall, the teaser retains the typical rom-com charm that aficionados of the genre will undoubtedly appreciate.

According to Paramount Press Express, the official description of the film is as follows:

“AT MIDNIGHT follows Alejandro (Diego Boneta, “Father of the Bride”), an ambitious hotel manager, and Sophie (Monica Barbaro, TOP GUN: MAVERICK), a movie star navigating Hollywood intrigues. He intends to create his own boutique hotel.”

The synopsis continues,

“She’s attempting to concentrate on her new superhero picture, “Super Society 3,” in the hopes of obtaining her own spinoff, but she discovers her co-star (and lover!) Adam (Anders Holm, “Workaholics”) cheating. When the filming takes them all to Alejandro’s hotel in Mexico, fate intervenes. Despite their very different circumstances, Alejandro and Sophie began to meet surreptitiously at Midnight…”

Viewers should anticipate a delightful and hilarious romantic comedy suited for casual viewing based on the trailer and description. It delves into a variety of issues, including love, ambition, and desire.

A short peek at the cast and crew of At Midnight

Monica Barbaro plays Sophie Wilder, a film actress who has just split up with her partner after he was found cheating on her. Sophie then meets a young and gorgeous hotel manager under very difficult circumstances, but the two end up falling in love. In the clip, Barbaro appears fairly great, effortlessly reflecting her character’s charm and energetic attitude. Fans may anticipate a remarkable performance from her in the flick. Monica Barbaro is most recognized for her roles in Top Gun: Maverick, Chicago Justice, and Splitting Up Together, to mention a few.

Diego Boneta, who plays Alenjandro, the hotel manager with whom Barbaro’s character falls in love, co-stars in a major role opposite Barbaro. One of the trailer’s distinguishing characteristics is Barbaro and Boneta’s remarkable onscreen chemistry. Other prominent film and television acting credits for Diego Boneta include Father of the Bride, Luis Miguel: The Series, and many more.
