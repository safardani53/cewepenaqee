FIFA 23 releases the full list of World Cup Heroes with four 90+ players

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, June 10, 2024

THE World Cup kicks off this week, and to celebrate FIFA 23 has released 14 new FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) Icons.

All dressed to represent Marvel superheroes in a new collaboration, FIFA 23 has released these cards available now in FUT packs.

The World Cup mode was introduced into the game last week, and a number of cards have been added for fans to play with.

There are 12 sets of cards being given away for supporters of certain countries, including one Icon and one current player.

Completing missions in World Cup mode will also reward you with up to 50 tokens you can swap for different World Cup players, Icons, and FUT packs.

Players from the newly released World Cup FUT Heroes squad are also available in exchange for 25 Tokens.

More in FIFA 23

They will appear in FUT packs, though it will be rare to find one in each pack.

There are a number of highly-rated players available, including Rudi Völler, Diego Forlán, Lúcio, and Jean-Pierre Papin.

All of these players come with a rating of 90 of above.

Here’s the full list of World Cup Heroes available in FIFA 23:
