Katy Perry GOOGLES herself and posts 'hot' throwback picture falling out of her top and flashing her

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

AFTER being whacked with insecurity Katy Perry has dealt with it the only way she knows how – by Googling “katy perry hot”.

Just after midnight on Tuesday, the 32-year-old singer Instagrammed a super sexy picture of herself in a push-up bra and thigh-skimming shorts, as she seductively looked into the camera while not so casually squeezing a lemon.

And while it’s not unusual for popstars to post racy images of themselves, Katy admitted to fans she only did it because her last posts didn’t exactly hit the spot.

She captioned the image: “Was feeling insecure about my last two posts so”, and presumably left the Google Image search “katy perry hot” in the frame on purpose.

Fans found the quirky singer’s honest upload hilarious and flooded the picture with comments telling her so, and also nudging her to charge her phone.
