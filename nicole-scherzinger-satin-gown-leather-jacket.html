Nicole Scherzinger teams a satin floor length gown with leather jacket as she launches her new perfu

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 24, 2024

NICOLE Scherzinger looked sensational in a floor length silk gown which she teamed with a leather jacket for the launch of her new perfume.

The X Factor judge wowed as she stepped out in the slinky dress to celebrate her new perfume, Chosen By Nicole Scherzinger, at The Dorchester Hotel in London.

The 39-year-old gave her look some edge by pairing her chic gown with a black leather jacket.

Her jacket was emblazoned with the quotes "I want it all" on the back with a hand making the 'peace' sign.

For the launch, Nicole posed in the stunning surroundings of The Dorchester Hotel and was surrounded by bottles of her latest fragrance.

As she left the bash, Nicole blew kisses to the cameras before heading home in a black cab.

Her stunning outing comes after Nicole branded Louis Walsh a "liar" after he accused her of covering up her real age.

The former Pussycat Doll took a swipe at her X Factor co-star after he recently claimed she is not really 39.

The Irishman stirred things up ahead of the launch of the 2017 series on Saturday and by claiming the stunning singer has been fibbing about her birthday.

Louis suggested Nicole is much older than 39, but the singer has now hit back and insisted she is not lying about her age.

She said: "I wish I was lying about my age, I have never lied about my age.

"I should have started lying a long time ago."
