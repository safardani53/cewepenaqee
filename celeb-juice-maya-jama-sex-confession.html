Celeb Juice viewers shocked as Maya Jama makes racy sex confession

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

CELEBRITY Juice viewers were left shocked after Maya Jama made a racy sex confession.

The 26-year-old presenter sent fans into a frenzy when she took part in a cheeky game on Thursday's show.

Maya joined guest Joe Swash and team captain Emily Atack, as they guessed which sexy word they were acting out while jumping on a trampoline.

Joe was in the hot seat as she tried his best to give the girls clues, after a few wrong attempts Maya correctly guessed "69".

The I'm a Celeb winner was thrilled that Maya had got the question right, meaning another point for their team.

But eagle-eyed viewers were quick to notice Maya's cheeky remark.

After guessing the sex position correctly, Maya said: "Love a 69".

The X rated comment did not go unnoticed by fans, with hot-under-the collar fans flocking to social media.

One ITV viewer wrote: "Maya Jama loves a 69 - brilliant."

Another shared: ""Just loved @MayaJama on Celebrity Juice taking it too far on the trampoline scene."

A third posted: "Maya Jama 'love a 69' ..f**k me she is absolutely perfect in every way!!"

Maya was recently left in a fit of jiggles as she wobbles around the floor in another hilarious game.

Leaving little to the imagination, with just a black bra under the sexy, sparkling number, Maya jiggles around on a wobble board.

The Wibbly Wobbly game sees ITV show host Keith Lemon ask Maya to describe a series of words for her teammates to guess as she is wiggled about.

Maya struggled to speak as she takes on a challenge in a see-through mesh top.

"I'm never going to be able to explain this," she shouts as she stumbles over her words while getting distracted by the vibrating plate.

After narrowing down the colour green, singer HRVY guesses the right answer as Maya throws herself off the board for "a breather".

Maya recently became a permanent panellist on Celebrity Juice and now features on the show every other week.

Speaking about the role, she said: "I never have any idea quite what's going to unfold on Celebrity Juice, but I do know it's always going to be hilarious.

"It’s a show I dreamed about going on when I was growing up and so I’m beyond thrilled to become a regular on the show and be given the opportunity to share the panel with some of my favourite people. Bang Tidy!"

The episode comes after Maya was forced to hit back at online trolls who said she had a nose like Michael Jackson.

She told MarieClaire.co.uk that she does not let trolls bother her and actually has "a laugh about" the mean things they have to say.
