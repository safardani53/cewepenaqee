IT doesn't matter how comfortable your clothes are if you still have to wear a bra underneath them.

That's why Primark fans are dashing to get their hands on a stylish top they won't ever have to wear one with.

The vest tops with built-in bra padding were shared on Instagram recently by @welovethehighstreet - but you'll have to be quick if you want to nab one.

The vest top is priced at just £10 and is available in a white cropped style - so it's perfect for summer.

The fashion fan shared a clip of the top along side the caption: "My prayers have been answered.

"Primark new in built in bra vest tops have landed."

One commented: "Why have these never been done before? So good."
