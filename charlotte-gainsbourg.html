Who is Charlotte Gainsbourg:

Charlotte Gainsbourg is a famous Movie Actress. She was born on July 21, 1971 and her birthplace is England. Charlotte is also well known as, French-English singer, songwriter, and actress who became famous for her roles in the films Paroles et Musique and The Cement Garden.

Charlotte is originated from England. She was nominated for the Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress for her work in Melancholia, which starred Kiefer Sutherland.
