Photos of Pop Icon &amp; Actress Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

Suzanne Somers always knew how to make a lasting impression. See some of the late actress’ best photos here.

Suzanne Somers was best known as an actress. She gained a huge spotlight when she appeared in the role of Chrissy Snow on the television sitcom ‘Three’s Company’ from 1977 until 1981. She went on to play the role of Carol Foster Lambert in the sitcom ‘Step by Step’ from 1991 until 1998 and has appeared in numerous other television shows and films.

Her celebrity status gave her an opportunity to compete on the reality show ‘Dancing with the Stars’ in 2015 and she also made a guest appearance on ‘The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’ two years earlier in 2013. She also did work as a model and appeared in various publications, including ‘Playboy’ and she became the spokesperson for the exercise product Thighmaster, for which she appeared in many infomercials.

Suzanne died at the age of 76 on October 15, 2023. She was battling two forms of cancer on and off for years before her death. Suzanne was surrounded by her family members when she passed away. To remember Suzanne, click through the gallery to see photos of the late actress.
